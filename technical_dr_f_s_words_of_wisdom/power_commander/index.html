The best way to find out if the bike has one it to inspect the big grey plug as people tend to hide the PC in different places on the SD.
You find this on the right hand side of the bike under the tank spoiler near the coolant tank. If you see two pairs of these the bike has a PC as it plugs in between the original which is a female/ male pair.
Haven't got a pic but you can't miss it
