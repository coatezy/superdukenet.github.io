On a dry sump bike I wouldn't think it would be a big problem as long as you haven't massively over filled the tank. If there wasn't enough room for the pump to return oil from the engine it could of course be a massive problem.

The Gin Reaper

2012-03-14 09:39:00 UTC

If the oil tank is over full the oil will dump out of the tank into the engine through the cam covers.
If the scavenge pump can't return it quickly enough I imagine it will come out through the crank case breather which on a stock bike will dump into the airbox and get burnt / vaporised.
I understand, however, that you can overfill by quite a bit before it becomes an issue. The SD's dry sump system tolerates overfilling much better than a conventional motor.
