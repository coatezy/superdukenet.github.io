I've had them fitted for about two months now. Great so far. Not been on track yet but I wanted some more road orientated tyres after 18 months on Racetecs which were brill in the dry but not good in the rain.

I like them a lot and think they probably offer all the grip I need and more.

The lean indicator stuff is really embarrassing though. I was showing Klink and Shade the new tread a few weeks ago and I was dreading the comments when they saw that stuff. Dreamed up by some teenage minded marketing idiots who have NO IDEA what we want on our tyres. I mean who would really ask for this stuff? Unless it indicated real hero status and like you say you can lean past the 5 without trying.

I think JehadJoe is running these too.
