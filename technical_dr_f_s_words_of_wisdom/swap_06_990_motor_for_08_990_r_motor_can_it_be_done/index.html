Assuming it's a pukka '08 SDR engine, off the top of my head you'll need to replace the ECU, speedo unit (only for the higher red line though I think), wiring harness and coil packs - would be worth a look at a dealer's part fiche, eg Jim Aim Racing here in the UK, which has a separate fiche for the R and suggests that, in addition to the engine, there may be some differences with the non-engine part of the oil system.

Have a look here www.rubbersideup.com for the parts you may need - have bought a few bits from them, great service, etc and the owner is/was a member here.
