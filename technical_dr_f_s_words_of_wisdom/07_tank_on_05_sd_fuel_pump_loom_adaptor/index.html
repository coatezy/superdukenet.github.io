In order to change the level sensor you have to take apart both fuelpumps. in the bottom of the fuelpump there is a connector,simply pull out the levelsensor on the 05 pump and insert it in the connector of the 07 pump,it should be the free slot just above the red wire.

If you want to make an adaptor you have to by the connectors first.These can be ordered from ktm as an original part.

on the 05 wire loom connetion for the fuelpump there are 3 wires, the red/brown is for the level sensor,the brown is positive ,and blue black is negative.
on the fuelpump red is positive and black is negative.the blue is for the levelsensor but it is not used on the 07 pump.
on the 07 fueltank levelsensor orange is positive and black is negative.

so in order to make the adaptor. the brown wire from the 3 pin connector on the 05 wireloom goes to the red wire on the 3 pin connector on the fuelpump and the blue/black goes to the black wire on the fuel pump.the blue/black is also used as negative wire for the 2 pin connector from the levelsensor.
the red/brown wire from the same 3 pin connector goes to the orange wire on the 2 pin connector from the levelsensor.

I hope this makes sense
blue wire in pic is not used
