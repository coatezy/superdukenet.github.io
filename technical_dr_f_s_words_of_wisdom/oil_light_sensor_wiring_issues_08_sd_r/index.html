2009-01-03 00:54:00 UTC

Well, my brand new 2008 SDR (100 miles on it so far) has had this problem from the very first ride 9last week). As soon as the bike got hot the oil light came on when idling, and went away when revving. Freaked the CRAP out of me, as I thought I had just run the first 50 miles of the bike's life while low on oil. Not a good way to start... the fact that the oil level sight seems to give almost random readings didn't help my confidence either...

Thanks all for this great forum! I found the information about the issue and I even learned how to (more or less) "read" the oil level. There's plenty of oil in the engine... the bike is good except for this small electrical gremlin. Whew!

I actually pointed my dealer towards these threads... I'll be taking the bike in as soon as they reopen next week and have the issue addressed. I'll also get the Akras and the new map on... I was going to wait until first service, but the fueling right now really is terrible (much worse than my other KTMs), and I am hoping the new cans + map will improve things. The bike also really feels chocked by the stock exhaust... I need to let the beast breathe!
