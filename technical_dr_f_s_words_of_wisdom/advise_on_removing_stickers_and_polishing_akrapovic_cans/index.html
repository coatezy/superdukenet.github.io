I have used goo gone to take those stickers off a set of Leo pipes, you an also use a hair dryer to heat it up a bit and it should lift up no problem. Use the goo gone to wipe of any residue stickiness and then use some auto glym metal polish to tidy up the pipe. It should work pretty well and it should be nice and shiny. Good luck.

I'm with Klinck on this. I used some 3M Scotchbrite pads to remove scratches that were fairly deep. Use the green ones for the fairly deep ones and then finish off with the white pads. For light scratches just use the white ones. No matter what though, plan on buffing the whole can to make it match because it will change the finish. Also, do not use steel wool as tiny fibers may get embedded and rust. I also used WD40 with the pads. Some like it, others advise against its use but I've noticed no problems.
