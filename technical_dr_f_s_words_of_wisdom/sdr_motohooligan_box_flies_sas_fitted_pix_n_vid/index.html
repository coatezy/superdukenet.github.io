SDR Motohooligan box, 'flies, SAS fitted (pix n vid)

ShadeTheChangingMan

2010-09-11 15:38:00 UTC

So, it arrived Friday and I couldnt wait so I was out there in my garage until it got too dark to see what I was doing (no power in my garage) fitting it. Finished it off this morning and it is very good. Quite easy to do in fact...apart from the RHS SAS cover....what a bitch to remove that was!! I was determined NOT to remove the rhs hanger to get to it and I think I tool marginally less time doing it my way.

Glad I removed the tank entirely too, uber easy job and really makes it easier to get at everything!! I took a few snaps along the way and a video of the bike running when I fired it up after installation. Audio isnt brilliant but the induction roar is there alright! I cant wait to rag this baby. Gotta book Dyno time asap and get it remapped. It chugs out 127bhp at the wheel in pre-MH form so am hoping to crack 130bhp after mapping.

Fingers crossed for some dry road time tomorrow. Should take the edge off not going to Enduroland with Ronin n Klincky. Talking of which....Ronin, I hope you bought that R mate....I dont wanna be waiting for you! [/url]
