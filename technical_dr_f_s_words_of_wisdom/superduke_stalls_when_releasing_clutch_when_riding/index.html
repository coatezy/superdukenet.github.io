Shiroyu

2014-06-05 17:27:00 UTC

I had something like this happen when I first bought my used SD. It happened twice and hasn't happened again in 20,000 miles of riding. Both times I was accelerating normally on a highway and shifting up in one of the higher gears. As I shifted the motor just quit as if the key had been turned off. I don't remember exactly what I did, but I got it going again without losing much speed - I think what I did was to cycle the key switch and bump start it at speed in a high gear.

It has never happened again and I don't know what caused it. It was scary for sure because the first time it happened I had just passed an 18 wheeler on a 2 lane highway. Would have been bad if it had happened then.

I asked the PO (a friend of mine) if it had ever happened to him and he said no.

Sorry I don't have an answer, but if you figure it out please let us know.
