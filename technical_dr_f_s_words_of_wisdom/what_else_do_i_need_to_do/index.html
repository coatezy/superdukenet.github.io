All comments and advice appreciated, cheers

2012-11-30 10:11:00 UTC

17/38 and 16/40 are absolutely not similar! 16/40 is much shorter, and gives less top speed compared to 17/38
For example: revs @ 120km/h 6th gear (according to gearing commander)
16/38 (stock) :4779
16/40 (fun) :5031
16/41(Wheelies) :5157
17/38 (stock on 05/06 ) :4498

To get close to 16/40 using a 17t front would require a 43t rear which would give you 5090 rpm in 6th @ 120km/h

2012-11-30 10:57:00 UTC

This is what I have done to mine and I'm very happy with it.

08 SD
CPR airbox
Yoshimura carbon exhaust
Remove SAS and 02's
Removed Secondary fly's
Custom dyno tune map (tune ecu)

126 rwhp

Absolutly love it. Keep in mind though removing the secondary fly's will cause the engine to choke a little in the lower revs at wide open throttle even with tuning compensation. But a much smoother bike without them. You loose a little low down grunt but gain more throttle control through and powering out of a corner. Less engine breaking also.
