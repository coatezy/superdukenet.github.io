I got some Leo Vince's recently after trawling Ebay etc. for months. A user on here was selling some so I bought them off him and got round to fitting them yesterday. Bloody hell they are a lot louder than the stock exhaust! They seem quite well designed really - not too much different from stock around town i.e. below 4k and on part throttle, just a bit more of a bassy tone. As soon as you open that throttle up properly though it is like being on a race bike! This is with the baffles in as well, god alone knows what they would be like without them. I might try it out sometime but it would have to be out in the countryside as I don't think my neighbours would be happy if I experimented at home.

Another big advantage is getting rid of the boat anchor that is the stock exhaust, I almost put my back out picking it up
