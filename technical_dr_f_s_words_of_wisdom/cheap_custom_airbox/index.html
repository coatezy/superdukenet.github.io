I always wanted to install better airbox so I ordered one from local composite workshop. Technician measured the space under tank and made the mold for this custom airbox. Results are here:




It is designed to use standard airfilter WA6383 available from most of the car parts shop and also ebay.
I cant say that it adds many hp-s (not dyno tested yet) but it makes my sd run much better and smoother + i can buy new airfilter if i want ( about 6 EUR), maintenance is easier and etc.

Price of this airbox was 185EUR, he can produce more if anyone is interested and the price for quantities more then 5 will be cheaper. Made in Estonia, price includes all taxes in EU.

Btw: This air box is sold in Europe since 2009:


The filter is a short K&N fitting under the tank of all 990-V2, from SD over SM up to Adventure. The cover is made from laser-cutted stainless steel. So it does not look so nice als cover made out of an ally billet, but on the other hand, it is easy to manufacture, so the price keeps within resonable limits: Complete air box kit, everything inside, ready to install - € 200,-- inkl. sales tax, free to your door within EC.

Of course this air box is dyno-tested: side by side, same motorcycle, same day, same dyno, one dyno session with this air box installed, one with pure and open velocity stacks in the atmosphaere - same output. ´This should give a prove that this box oofers the engine what it need for optimal power production.
