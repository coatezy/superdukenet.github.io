I have the ventura rack (with standard exhaust).

The rack could be modified if you are a neat welder and has access to oxy or similar to allow a couple of bends - so dont write them off completely... Im looking to get mine changed so that the mounting brackets bend inwards to avoid the exhaust - have had it looked at by a couple of mates who are welders by trade.

There seems to be no competetion for the Ventura, and they have no plans to make one that is more aftermarket friendly.
