Superduke R Seat, retro fit kit.

Dr F

2007-07-10 16:26:00 UTC

So, after ten weeks on backorder, my SD R seat kit has finaly arrived! In case you have been on the Moon, or trapped under something heavy for the last few months, this kit alows you to retro fit the SD R seat unit to the '05/'06 Superduke. There is also a kit to fit the '07 with a different part number. The cost is Â£406 inc vat, unless you have a friendly dealer like me, who gives a 10% discount, which makes it a little more easy to digest. Especialy in the light that the seat unit comes unpainted. It's supplied in a flat black bare plastic, which would have been ok, had they not finnished it ready to paint, ie, with polish marks in it where they've flatted it off. Gonna speak to a painter mate of mine about painting it satin black, but will probably run it as is, until winter. So it's off to the garage for the rest of the evening, there are over 40 parts apparently, so I guess it won't be a ten minute job!
