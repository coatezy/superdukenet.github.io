The manual says 2,5 litres then add necessary amount with warm engine until max mark on sight tube. When you put in 2,5 litres it stays in oil tank, then when you fire it up engine sucks it into clutch, oil filter, transmission etc. I managed to add 2,5 litres plus 1,7 litres - total 3,7 litres and it was above max mark when hot. Today after 400km I sucked out about 150ml and it is exacltly on max mark on sight tube. So it takes about 3,5 litres actually, at least mine did it exactly in such amount.
