Kevxtx SMR/SMT Mod book

kevxtx

2013-01-12 06:42:00 UTC

A Complete guide to modifications, performance enhancements, including performance exhausts, air filters, fuel injection, Tune ECU, Power Commander maps encyclopedia, all the data available in one place. The book contains a complete modification index in PDF format, the complete book has over 400 pages of pictorial information with detailed information on certain subjects. I have tried to add as much information in areas where the information was available, I would like to thank everyone who contributed their info to us on the net, all I have done is put it all in one place. All the mods have actually been carried out with proven results.

This is the 3 modification index I have written on different bikes, the aim of the mod book is to help others & the download is for free.

Please note: This mod book is an English only version.

You will need a Adobe reader or Word to view this book.
