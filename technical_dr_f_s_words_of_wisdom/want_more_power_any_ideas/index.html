do not dismiss gear ratio change... unless you can hit rev limitter in 6th gear, you will not loose top speed, and gain loads of acceleration...
what is your top speed now?
i gained top speed when i went from 16/38 to 16/40, as weird as it sounds... and i have exact same setup as you, except Ti Leo's...

i regularly ride with a buddy on GSXR1000, and up to ~200km/h he cannot gain any ground on me (he's also ~70lb heavier...), and in the twisties, he can hardly keep up.

i'd suggest 16/39 gearing + custom map; if that is not enough, then you got a wrong bike for your needs - sell it and go get a b-king - i guarrantee you won't have problems with catching fz1....
