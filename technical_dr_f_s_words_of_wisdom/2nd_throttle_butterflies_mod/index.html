2015-05-27 13:46:00 UTC

I took my 2nd flys out, since it seemed like that was the generally accepted thing to do. However when I had my bike custom mapped by a dyno tuner he said I should have left them in. He told me that when properly mapped by a tuner that knows what he's doing the 990 can be made to run smoother and stronger with the flys than without. He said it's a pretty small difference so I decided not to bother with putting them back in. But if I had it to do over I'd leave them alone I think.

kevxtx

2015-06-12 12:18:00 UTC

My understnding from doing this on a first Gen Yam FZ was that the flies helped create a turbulence that improved intake velocity, but the gains were small and primarily top end. The concern in the SD with no flies has been stumbling with WOT at low speeds because of the nature of the intake velocity of a large bore vtwin, but to my knowledge, nobody has every reported this. I run Motoflies in the Duchess, but can't tell you it gives me any advantage as I have never run her with the flies out.
