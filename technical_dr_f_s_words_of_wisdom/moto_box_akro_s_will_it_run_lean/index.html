I took my SDR to be dyno set-up with; Akra Evo slip on's, motobox, M/H secondary flies, standard ECU mapping and a Dynojet map already loaded onto the PC3 (for slip-ons). Believe it or not, the map I ended up with after the set-up was actually leaner in general than the one I took it in with. The dyno operator stated that the A/F ratio was virtually perfect all the way though the rev range after leaning it out.
