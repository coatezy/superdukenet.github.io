Grendel

2011-06-27 01:08:00 UTC

The Evap system is a fuel recovery system and would have no effect on the tune on the ECU. Grendel doubts there is any difference from a tune perspective between US and Euro models.

Jermo

2011-06-27 01:19:00 UTC

When loading a different mapping in the ECU itself this should be the same as with european models.

With a PC or RB however there is a difference because those devices only strengthen or weaken the output signal from the ECU before it gets to the injectors. If this ECU output signal is different (US vs EU) this would mean the pre-loaded mapping has a different effect. When tuning on the dyno however this shouldn't matter of course.
