The DNA produced the best gains. Noticeably better than the Motobox in both feel and dyno-proven. I bought my DNA3 direct.
As regards the DNA v the Motohooligan v, etc. I know some people did get quite excited in their defense / attack of one box or another.
All I can say with regards to the DNA3 i was personally satisfied that the DNA3 was worth the extra money.
On Kev's dyno we were also satisfied that the performance of the DNA matched our best case scenario making almost 5bhp more than our modded airbox. The DNA3 made the same power as no airbox at all.
I am hoping to realise the full benefits of the increased air flow capacity with the tuned motor.
So, in a word: recommended.
