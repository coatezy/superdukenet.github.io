Does anyone know if this needs a tube?

JohnJJr

2013-06-19 22:04:00 UTC

I had my petrol tank up last week whilst making adjustments to my throttle bodies and left it that way for an hour of so whilst I had my lunch. When I returned I was aware of a petrol smell coming from the garage and when I checked it out there was a small puddle of petrol on the floor.
To cut a long story short I identified this connector at the bottom of the tank as the culprit; dripping every so slightly but only when in the upright position.
Question is, is it supposed to weep fuel when the tank is in this position as observed and should it have a tube attached (can't see that there ever was one)?
