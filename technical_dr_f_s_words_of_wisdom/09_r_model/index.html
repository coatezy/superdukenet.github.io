Colonel_Klinck

2013-07-12 13:35:00 UTC

The subframe is the same as the standard and you can swap to dual seat. You'll need a few bit but they can all be bought.

08 onwards SDRs do have Akra headers as standard

I've owned 06 SD and 08 SDR and have to say the 06 is a far more pleasant ride in town. SDR is hard work due to the engine throttle response. When you're on it on the roads or on track the SDR comes into its own though.
