I want to shave 63kg off my bike this year.
That would make bike and rider (without gear) about 275kg
If the bike is making 130bhp the power/weight ratio at the moment is 130/307 = 0.42
If I loose the 5st (63kg) then the figure is 130/244 = 0.53
Or an improvement in overall power to weight ratio of 26%
