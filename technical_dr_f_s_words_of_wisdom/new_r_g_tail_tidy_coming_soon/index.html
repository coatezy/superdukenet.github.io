Yep that would be me. There are good guys down at TriCounty.

I don't know if you noticed how I had chopped down my tail but if R&G are going to make something it could be worth telling them.

Basically the number plate/indicator bracket has 4 bolts that hold it to the mudguard. Remove that and cut/trim the mudguard from the tail light back and you then have the original 4 bolt holes to mount a new bracker to. THis results in a very agressive looking rear end..

Having done this the only challenge I have is indicators. I was thinking of modifying the original light and getting some LED's built in to it.

If R&G make a decent bracket that can take mini-inditcators that might be an easier option.
