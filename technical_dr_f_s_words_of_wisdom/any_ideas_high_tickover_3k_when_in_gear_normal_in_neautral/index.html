Update.... been for a good run this afternoon...mixed riding, tried a lot of in town stuff to keep the engine hot, and no problems to report.
Checked the usual suspects for high idle and the only thing I could find wrong was possibly the clutch switch.. to be more accurate the small metal contact strip under the handle bar control. Pulling the clutch in was making the contact with the metal bar but I could not hear the click of the contact within the switch.... moving the bar with my finger towards the contact gave the click, click.....very carefully bent the bar towards the lever which then gave a better contact at the lever and when the lever was pulled all the way back I could then hear the click of the switch.
Looks like this may have been the problem, as the electric wizardry was not realizing the the clutch was engaged and setting idle mode.
10 points goes to no47 for his input and thanks to all who suggested possible causes
