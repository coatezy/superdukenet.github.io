Locating a Voltage power wire

Bink

2008-12-07 18:56:00 UTC

Hi folks, Any resident electricians on board?
I'm in the process of fitting some Symtec Heated grips and need a bit of help locating a voltage power wire.
The instructions tell me to use a wire such as one leading to the headlight or dashlight to connect the heated grips power wire to.

My problem is that there are 3 wires going into the dash & headlight so I do not know which of these is the power wire.
Also like some advise on which one to use. I'm thinking that using the headlight wire would mean that the grips only work when the lights are switched on.
Plus for safety I'd prefer to use the dashlight. If the grips do cause a short I'd rather ride with no dash then no headlight.

I do plan to installl a 'In-Line Blade Fuseholder' between the heated grips and the (to be used) voltage power wire, just to be sure. The instructions tell me to use a 4 or 5 amp fuse.

In case it matters, the bike is a 05 model.

Thanks for your help.

Aegis Bearing Mel

2008-12-07 19:20:00 UTC

There should be auxilary feeds under the headlight mask with spade connectors bud.
One is live all the time, the other is a switched feed.
I've mine connected to the switched feed, seems to do the trick nicely.
Only word of warning I would give is to watch out, as my battery died and couldn't keep the digital Oxford grips on...
