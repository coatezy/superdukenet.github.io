It doesn't matter which terminal you remove, but it's always best to remove the negative. If you remove the positive, when it comes to reconect, if you accidentaly touch any (metal) part of the bike with the spanner when tightening the bolt, you'll have a whole load of sh*t on yer plate! Not that you'll be able to see it, 'cos you will have been blinded by the big fuggin' flash! Remove the negative, it's safer.
