jambox

2014-05-02 16:16:00 UTC

What year is your bike? Different years had different sprockets as standard.

The 05/06 boys pretty much always go -1 on the front to a 16t, which is what came as standard on 07+

As for the rear, I believe a popular choice is 40t. I myself am still running the 38t, but there are some running 42+. There are a few threads on peoples experimentation on the different gearing, which may help you choose.
