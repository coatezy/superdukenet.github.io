SDRK, I'm surprised to hear that you managed to use slipons with the full system pipework as I've been told that the diameters are different. The dirtbikebook site at has two different versions. The slip ons to go on the standard exhaust are:

990 SUPERDUKE 05 - 06 AKPRAPOVIC EVOLUTION SLIP ON END CANS
Mounting only with original headers possible.

And the end cans for the full system are:

990 SUPERDUKE 05 - 07 EVOLUTION SILENCERS
62505099100 without catalytic converter with EC certificate for model 05-06, also usable for 07-08 model but without EC certificate ( e.g. for Racing ).
Mounting only possible in combination with akrapovic headers 61005007200 or 61005007300 .

Also on this topic KTMax suggests that the diameters are different too, as do two of the dealers I have asked this of in the past.

Did you have any trouble mating the two?
