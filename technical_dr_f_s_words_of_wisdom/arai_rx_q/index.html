I just tried one out. It is a superbly built helmet, but I find that unless I tilt my head forward like I am looking at the speedo, or tuck way down below my little skidmarx screen, the vents on the top of the helmet whistle like crazy. I don't mean just a little wind noise, I mean it sounds like winter wind howling across the plains. Too bad as it's a great helmet in every other way (other than the visor changing mechanism). I think I'm going back to Shoei or AGV.
