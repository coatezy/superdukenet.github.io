I had a read of that repair howto on the Adventurer site and decided to
give it a go. Yes, the water pump seal is definately shit itself, so it's fault
confirmed !

confirm the fault and so that I can take the faulty parts into the dealer for
a match up (dealers here won't refund their fuckups once the parts
leave their store).

I didn't follow this guys sequence. I built the special tool to remove
the impeller (as shown in the guys howto), however, if I had undone it
using this, I would have broken the impeller since the bolt was extremely
welded by the loctite. So, I made a rectangular key (connects to the engine
side of the impeller shaft) so I could crank the bolt using a normal ratchet.
Basic

A silly question: Why not crank the impeller bolt when it's connected to the
drive assembly ?. The coupling to the crank is all metal and you can't break
any plastic. I don't understand why the guy made special tool to crank it ?.
Am I missing something ?
