Running the Standard or Soft settings per book (sans preload front and rear). Surprised the toolkit doesn't have a 24mm for the fork preload, I don't even have one of those. Going to try to pick one up and muck about with front and rear static sag.

I ride the SD ever' day and find the sport settings a bit on the harsh side. The soft/standard is a bit better, but I think compression is still a bit much.
