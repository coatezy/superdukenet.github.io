Everybody knows Dynos vary greatly, but I just had mine tuned yesterday. FMF Pipes (inserts in), PCIII, BMC, and SAS blocked off.

Got 112 HP and 68 lb/ft on a known-to-be conservative dyno. I didn't dyno it before installing the pipes and filter, but the baseline A/F was way too rich (figured it would be lean) and the curve had more dips and valleys than the Alps. Now my torque curve looks like a tabletop and I picked up 10 HP at 6000 RPM just from the tuning.

She runs real nice now.
