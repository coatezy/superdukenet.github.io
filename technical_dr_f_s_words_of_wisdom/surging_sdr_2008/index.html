The trouble with KTM and the superduke is that the maintenance schedule does not call for synchronizing or balancing the throttle bodies. I dont know why. Every japanese bike calls for synchronizing those 4 carbs or throttle bodies at every service including the first. Read all the posts from members who claim the problem disappeared after their first service. they apparently had dealers who balanced the throttle bodies. BELIEVE ME, I have balanced the throttle bodies and the bike now runs perfectly. Like its supposed to. check the thread titled surging problem solved.
