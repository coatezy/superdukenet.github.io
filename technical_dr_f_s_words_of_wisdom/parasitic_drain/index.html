As some will know I've spent a fair bit of time mucking about with speedo's of late. There is an interesting phenomenon
on the 07+ speedo's - if you plug them in with the ignition off they sometimes whir and sometimes the F1 light comes on.
This doesn't happen all the time and appears related to swapping from one speedo (<07) to a >07.

The bottom line is that the 07+ clearly has power and does some sort of powering on routine even with the ignition off.
Thus there probably is some low level drain.
