If it's a Gipro unit, it will be connected behind the headlight between two white 3 pin connectors.
There will also be a single wire going to a white/blue wire coming from the speedo.
If that's the case, here is a manual with the setup instructions:
https://www.dropbox.com/s/nsg3mf0vzjwbk ... n.pdf?dl=0

The Gipro uses revs and speed to calculate the gear the bike is in.
