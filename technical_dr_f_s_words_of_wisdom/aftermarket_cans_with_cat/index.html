Twisted Jester

Hi folks, looking for some collective wisdom.

I have HP Corse cans. I'm not totally satisfied with their sound. Without the db killers they sound awesome but too loud. With the db killers, even after modification, they're ok but not great.

I'm looking for cans with a deep sound but not too loud. I was looking around for alternatives and saw that there are Akras and HPs with catalizers available. These would also have the advantage of not impregnating my clothes with exhaust smell, I suppose.

Does anyone have experience with these? I'm interested to know how they perform as far as power, sound and smell go.

Thanks in advance.

Seems to me lime The best alternative would be The Akras without db killers. I've Heard that setup and it sounded decent. Only downsides are the cost, The smell and The "inferior looks" compared to the HPs, to m'y eyes anyway.

I'm wondering though how the HPs would sound without the DB killers, but with the catalyzer. Maybe they would retain their deep rumble but with a lower noise level, due to the cat. Anyone ever tried that setup. Back when I bought them I don't think they had a catalyzer option. It's not clear also if the catalyzer option ca be retrofited. Anybody here understand Italian?

As far as smell is concerned, I thought that all aftermarket cans were equal, since they don't have a catalyzer. I'm running the Akra map for slip-ons. Is there another mapping that would help cure the smell?

I only use the HPs without the DB killers when on track. On the street they're too loud. Last year there was a track where they had a noise limit. I was afraid I was going to be too loud without the DB killers. If my memory serves me right, the limit was 98 DB and I measured in at 96, so I was ok.
