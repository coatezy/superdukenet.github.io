2016-06-19 07:40:00 UTC

Using that vacuum gauge will pull air in through the thread of the bleed nipple. The nipple only seals on the seat of the banjo bolt and the end of the nipple, not on the threads of the nipple. As soon as that seal is open the threads will pass air. The vacuum will just help it passed.

Using just a bleed tube and patience won't allow air down the thread as your pressurising from the insidewith fluid so lim gas you get you opening, closing and lever action correct. the reverse bleed method will also keep air out as you pressurise with oil from outside in.
