I disconnected my 02 sensor before I left work today. I commute about 40 miles each way. Let me tell you, it was definitely smoother and a lot less lurchy. It seemed like I didn't have as much pickup and go (torque), but it also didn't decelerate as abruptly. My bike definitely felt different and I will now have to get use to it. Yes the FI light came on.

Overall I think it’s a step in the right direction as far as comfort goes. I don't hit the track, but I hit the twisties everyday as part of my commute. Key word comfort. I would recommend this mod for regular street use.
I decided to order the G2 throttle cam and the 02 eliminators.

I have stock everything except for the evap can removed and the 2nd gear limiter removed.
