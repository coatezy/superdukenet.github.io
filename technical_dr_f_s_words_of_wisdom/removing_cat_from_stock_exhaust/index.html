the standard cats can be burnt out with a cutting torch but its a vicious job.Hang the cat entrance down and use a cutting torch that is or has been straightened insert and heat up and melt away.Wear real hand protection as every thing falls past your hands and move far from everything - when it hits the ground it pops runs exsplodes etc.You will no it melts and forms lumps wh remelt and if does not touch another peice falls out or connects on contact and has to be remelted. Its like a few hrs work but can open rather well. Does not have the best air flow but feels and sounds a lot better.
