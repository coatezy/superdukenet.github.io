Plipton

2014-04-20 20:34:00 UTC

Sounds like a clock fault or wiring/plug not quite located properly. Check the big plug is in place properly before doing anything else and that no other wiring (particularly the ground connections) has not been disturbed when you changed the mask. I assume the 1,2, 3 etc reading is in the units part of the display and not the tens??

FWIW, I had a similar issue when I put carbon wheels on my 2005 SD which, IIRC, was due to the speedo pickup being too close to the disk bolts. Unless you've also changed the wheels and/or brakes then obviously this does not affect your bike.
