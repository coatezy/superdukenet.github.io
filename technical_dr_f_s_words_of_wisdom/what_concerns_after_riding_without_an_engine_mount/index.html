I posted this question in another thread but I think it got lost in the posts.

I recently bought an 08 SD from a shop that had removed the frame sliders and forgot to replace the engine mount bolt used for the lower slider. I rode the bike twice in this condition, both very tame rides due to cold temps and road conditions. Is there anywhere in particular that I should be looking for damage, having had ridden the bike twice without this mount in place? As I look at the manual I can see that there are only three engine mounts; this one in question, the one at the swingarm pivot point and the support just a few inches forward of that.

I must be missing something because I can't see how the engine would stay in place while being ridden with only those low, rear mounts holding everything up!
