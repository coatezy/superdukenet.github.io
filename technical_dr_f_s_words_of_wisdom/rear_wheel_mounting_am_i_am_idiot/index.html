Rear Wheel Mounting - Am I am Idiot?

wacky_woodchuck

2013-08-12 03:01:00 UTC

Okay, this isn't my first bike, but I feel like a complete idiot here.

So I mounted new tyres and doing a general go through of my new to me used SD. Well I pulled the rear wheel, installed a new tyre and went to put it on and noticed the chain adjusters were locked solid. I got them sorted, went to install the rear wheel and I cannot get it on. I read and reread the manual and no dice.

It has a 16F/43R and 108 link chain. The adjusters were in the middle of their travel before I worked them free and chased then never-seized. Now I have the adjusters all the way in and I have no room on the chain side to get the adjuster blocks installed. See photo.



This is the closest I can get it pulling the wheel backwards.

My step to reinstall was:

Line up the brake, install chain, slide axle through and bam! Struck. I didn't bash anything or force it. I just kept uninstalling and trying to reinstall. I tried to install the axle then the chain, nope. Not happening.

I wouldn't be so concerned if the adjusters weren't in the middle and the chain had good slack BEFORE I installed a new tyre.
