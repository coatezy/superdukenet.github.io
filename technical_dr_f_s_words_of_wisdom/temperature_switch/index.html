Good idea! I looked in it before but couldn't find one in NZ that fitted and since it's never that hot there I gave up.
Now that I moved to OZ I was going to look in to it again.
The tread is the most important thing as the wire on the SD just has 2 connectors on it, that can be easily changed.
It is not the same plug, connector btw.

Tip: If you want to try the switch, tilt the bike over a bit more to the left. Get a friend to help or suspend from strap. This will prevent getting much air in to the system as we all know the SD cooling system is an (#&@^!(^# to bleed.
