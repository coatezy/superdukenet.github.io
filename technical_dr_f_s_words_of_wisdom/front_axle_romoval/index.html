Axle removal tool?

If you want to take of the front wheel, place front of bike on jack up stand, take of brake calipers, undo the nut on the end of axle (cant remember if its allen key or 32mm spanner) then undo the 4 the small allen key clamps, give it a little tap with rubber mallet or your hand and should slide out without drama.

That Allen key is just an end cap that holds the axle in the correct position. Just crack the joint
then loosen the four fork allen keys which grip the axel then the thing should slide out without any real force.
DON'T try to get the axle out without loosening the four allen bolts
