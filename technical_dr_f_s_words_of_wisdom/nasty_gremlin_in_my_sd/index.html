Bink

2011-01-31 20:10:00 UTC

I'm not sure but it sounds like electrical to me.

When I experienced an erratic dash it was down to an loose wire to the battery. You sure you checked the battery connections properly?
Did you take a reading off the battery to check the voltage?

It could be down to water or dampness in the electric system? (due to a recent hose down, wash or rain)
It's worth checking out the BGC (Big Grey Connector) which sits behind the coolant reservoir, on the RHS, give it a good dosing with silicon spray.

Check the negative connectors, if they are loose/broken this are the symptoms. They issue can be anywhere from the negative of the dash to the connectors of the battery and on frame connections.
If not, it is likely a rectifier regulator issue.
