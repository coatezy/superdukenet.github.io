I could not figure out why it would not fire so one of the technicians I work with took over. It is up and running now. But I did not have any time to talk to him about what he changed. Tuesday morning I will get more answers. He is the best mechanic I have ever met and has an electrical engineering degree. Needless to say it took him 3 hours to splice in the coils. The mysterious third wire coming from the coils is the ground. This is because the coil on an 08 SDR grounds off of the secondary side of the coil instead of the primary unlike every other bike (except maybe the Ducati). Now on to the next hurdle...
