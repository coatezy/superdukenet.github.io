1290 Heated Grips Tip

cessnatpa

2014-12-10 21:28:00 UTC

I recently installed heated grips and noticed a potential problem with the clearance of the plastic wire holder when the wheel is turned fully right. It's not likely anyone would be throttling up while doing a hard right turn but if you did and the steering stop is not adjusted properly it would break the plastic..

Also my left handle bar was only tapped for one screw for holding the grip and had to drill and tap the 2nd screw on top, anyone else have this issue?
