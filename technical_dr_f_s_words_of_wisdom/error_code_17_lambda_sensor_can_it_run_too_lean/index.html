jambox

2012-04-29 15:43:00 UTC

Yeah pretty much. Turning them off in TE is the same as fitting the O2 eliminator with a power commander. In order to fit them you disconnect the sensors.

The only difference you have by just disconnecting them is the FI light telling you there is a fault, ie they're not connected. The eliminator is fitted to trick the ECU into thinking that they are still fitted/working correctly.

In short, yes disconnect them and ignore the light.
