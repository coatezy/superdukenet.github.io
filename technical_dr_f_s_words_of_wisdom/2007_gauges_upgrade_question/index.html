Hi, I had exactly the same problem, symptoms as you describe. I drilled a little hole in the side of the clear plastic casing (covered with tape when not venting) but didn't fix the problem as my facing was also cracked.

I asked the shop for a quote for the new clocks; they are about 270 pounds in the UK and they estimated 3 HOURS labour to fit them.
I bought some new clocks on EBay, they looked mint but the seller could not guarantee whether they worked at all, got the for 70 pounds.

I installed them myself, having never undone the front before, in abut 30 mins. The new version clocks are exactly plug and play, they seem much better built for water resistance.

recommend getting the new clocks and perfectly easy to fit yourself.

Woah, woah, woah before you buy or do anything to modify your current gauges... Take it to the dealer. No, not kidding. I took mine in and like many others on here were given the new updated gauges for the cost of install/reprogramming. Totaled to ~$50.

I just mentioned that they were on the fritz and a friend of mine had the same issue (being all of you guys ) and asked if there was a service bulletin out for them. My dealer contacted KTM USA and the gauges were 'good-willed'. I, personally, wouldn't buy the same set you're using now as they're renowned for breaking... again.

This JUST happened about a week ago, so if the dealer tries to charge you full price I'd say it's them trying to get some money out of you.

Good luck!
