950smr's don't come with them bars or barkbusters.

They have a smaller tank than their equivalent 950sm.

They have pretty paint.

Other than that they're the same. Black calipers, swingarm etc aside.

If you're in the UK get the bike to X-bikes, if not buy one of his kits and install it.

Great bikes... i've owned 2.
