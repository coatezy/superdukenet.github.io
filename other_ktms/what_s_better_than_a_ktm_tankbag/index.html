What's better than a KTM tankbag....

weeksy

2008-08-19 13:43:00 UTC

Fitted my top box for Spa today as a tester... well blow me down... it's not bad

Fitting was a bit of messing about and working out which bit goes where... but it all works and fits up like a dream in the end.

I got the 37L on which is bloody HUGE !!!! carried a load of stuff in it on the way home on the twisties and it doesn't really seem to affect handling at all. HAd it up to 110 and no wobble/weave and tried with hard acceleration too and that was fine. Cornered at 95 and that went OK.

My only worry is it coming off !!!! LOL I think it's secure... but what do i know LOL i might have fitted it completely wrong !
