I still write a lot of crap. I just do it on here now

I do like the screenwriting courses though I have done several. Trouble is I feel a bit of a fraud as mostly they are full of young writers who want to progress and make a career of it.

Same feeling when I go in fast group on trackdays and find it is full of club racers. I've been there years ago and found out I didnt have it. Now it is just fun to me. But almost everyone else out there is of the 'kill em and eat em' mentality. Now they get a kick out of getting past me and I get a kick out of staying out of the gravel.

I do think I have, maybe, one great screenplay in me. But every year that goes by I get less inclined to put it out there and find out. I guess, for most of us life is better lived with the 'what could have beens' than cope wth the rejection.
