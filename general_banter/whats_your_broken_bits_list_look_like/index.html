Whats your broken bits list look like?

Mr KTM

2008-11-25 21:18:00 UTC

First up this is definitely not a whinge about Superdukes thread. As I abso-frikking-lutely love mine Rather I was curious to see what you guys have had break or malfunction on your SD's. (original parts only)

My broken bits list.. EDIT updated now

- Clutch slave piston broke
- O ring on oil filler plug squared off and had to be replaced
- gasket between rear header pipe replaced twice
- rear header pipe flange that sits into the header snapped off the short pipe
- stock pipe split 8cms where the s-bend is under the engine
- oil pressure switch failed
- coolant leak under resevoir tank where small hose attatches
- water pump under warranty
- oil pump under warranty
- starter motor under warranty


Seems a lot, but I love that bright orange bitch waaay too much to even say a bad word about it

All the above, well, tis a small price to pay for what it gives me in return
