For the first time in 4 1/2 yrs I am sans SD.

ShadeTheChangingMan

2011-11-21 17:00:00 UTC

Yes gents and ladies. I sold the R today. it is a sad day but a day I chose nonetheless. I've barely ridden it since Cadders, in fact the only time has been to get it MOTd. I could have ridden it once of twice in that time but my dirt bikes are currently fave round these parts and I have only about 20% of the free time I used to have.

So, for the 1st time in 4.5 years and about 60000 miles I am without a SuperDuke. Luckily I still have my 450 EXC Racing to twist the grip on and a shiny new set of titanium headers waiting to be fitted to it should ease the pain.

I didnt get top dollar for it but I dont regret cancelling that ebay auction where it only hit Â£4550! I've striped the bling and virtually sold all of it already barring the coupla bits on here (see the right section).

One of the guys from Cadders had dibs on my wheels....will be digging out his PM in a mo (I cant remember who it was) and let him know they are up for grabs. So dont bother PMing me via this thread...if anything else goes up for sale I will be posting on here about it

Fear not mes amis! I shall return to road riding one day. I still lust after a big SM (but not too big) just to try something different and I aint leaving the worlds greatest forum either.

One thing is for sure....whatever happens, whatever bike i get....you wont find me on here slagging off the R like an ex smoker does about ciggies. It is an AWESOME machine. I loved it so. But times change. II didnt sell it cos I was fed up with it or wanted something instead. I just wasn't getting to use it.
