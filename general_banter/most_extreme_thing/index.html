One time, many years ago, I played a Playstation game for an entire day. I woke up and put it on, played for an hour, had breakfast, played for a few hours, had a bath, played more, had lunch. Then I played it more before dinner. After that I played it all night until going to bed again at about 4am. In all I clocked up 15 hours gameplay of that masterful game. I had no contact with any other person that day, I was totally focussed on my goal and in a state of zen-like meditation for much of the day. Truly, it was a legendary day and one I'll never forget. Chances like that dont come by very often in life, you've got to grab them when they pass by or they will be gone forever. Pushing the limits of endurance, mental focus, emotional strength are what let you know you really are alive. Character building challenges make life a better experience and set a good example to others who lack the confidence to push hard.



It was Final Fantasy VII.



When I went to bed, I could see the screen imprinted on my retinae.
