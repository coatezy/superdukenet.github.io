Kawasaki mirrors on the SD

bic_bicknell

2013-02-23 13:15:00 UTC

I've had my digital camera/screen set up for a year or so but decided to go back to real mirrors. Hate the Superduke mirrors, especially the shape and rubber sleeves which split at the bottom. Been looking for ages for a set that suit the SD 'edge' styling and saw the Kawasaki Z750 at the London bike show last weekend. Quick internet trawl and I have a set of genuine Kwak mirrors. I think they suit the bike and just returning from a blast in the freezing weather this morning they work really well too.
