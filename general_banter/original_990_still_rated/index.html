bic_bicknell

2016-05-19 14:28:00 UTC

I agree. I keep on walking past 1290s in the local showroom and I just can't get enthusiastic about the design or the way they are built. My 990, like all KTMs made last decade, is pretty brutal and agricultural - but in a good way. It's made to not break like an off-road bike. The new 1290 is all plastic and cheap feeling with over-styled parts that just seem a bit tacky to me. Undoubtably a better performing bike than the old 990 but it doesn't make me want to own one and there is more to owning a bike than performance statistics.

Anyway, I'm not having a go at 1290s just glad to see that the 990 is still recognised as a great, iconic bike and I somehow doubt that the 1290 will be held in as high regard in 15 years time.
