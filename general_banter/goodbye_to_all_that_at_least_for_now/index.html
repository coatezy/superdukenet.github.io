Goodbye To All That (At Least For Now)

shadowman

2016-03-30 19:00:00 UTC

As some of you may have seen I sold my SD to Scotty and am now Superdukeless for the first time in a very long time.

My plan is to re-join the fold when circumstances permit but for now I will be laying low for a while.

Didn’t want to disappear without saying thanks to everybody that has helped with advice and support along my SD journey. I think more by luck than judgement I stumbled into one of the best bike forums around. Some great people here and, for me at least, a strong part of the enjoyment that SD ownership brought.

Still don't think there is a more amusing two wheel option out there for enjoyment on road and track!

Not sure what capacity or exact type of SD it will be but I expect to be back at some point and I may buy a cheap track bike in the mean time if I can just to do Crashfest as it would be good to catch up with the usual suspects.

Happy riding everyone….

PS: Anybody wishing to be the fastest SD this year better look out for a black and white bike that I suspect will be doing a lot of overtaking (Scotty is unlikely to be slow!)
