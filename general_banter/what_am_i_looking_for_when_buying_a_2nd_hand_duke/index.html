Superdan

2012-01-27 15:08:00 UTC

They are fairly good really, some on here with 47,000miles + with out major issues.

When you start it it up the toque limiter on the starter (later models were redisigned) make a nasty noise (sometimes) but no ill effects.

You can get the odd leak here and there on cylinders if your not careful so take a good look, when your getting to 15,000miles the water pump generally needs replacing as the seal goes 100euro job at home if your mechanically minded.

Dont think many people have had issues with head bearings etc.

But just like any bike, check service history, both keys damage etc.

Good luck

Mine had 36,000 miles on it when I flogged it. I had it regularly serviced at an independent, not even KTM and the only things beyond routine servicing were clutch slave cylinder (replaced under warranty) - if the example you're looking hasn't had this done, it'll need it sooner or later and I needed new front wheel bearings at about 20,000 miles. But I rode the bike all year round and the salty winter crap was what did for them.
Otherwise all good!
