ShadeTheChangingMan

2011-07-21 08:49:00 UTC

Just got back from the show. Mars Volta was great, but they only played for about an hour. Sound was crisp, epic jam session, their new drummer is awesome.

Soundgarden was super epic. About 60% of the songs were from Badmotorfinger, 30% from Superunknown, and 10% from that last album no one remembers just before they broke up. Matt Cameron is still easily the best drummer that emerged from the Seattle scene. Kim's guitar playing was good, nothing really blew my mind. Chris actually played a lot of the solos. Ben's bass playing was sort of like, yeah he's here because he is part of the original band. I think Kim covered a lot of his bass lines.

It was difficult to determine who won the Cat Wailing Vocalist award. When Cedric would wail it was mesmerizing, but when Chris started wailing the mountains started to crack. Probably because when Soundgarden took the stage the volume got about 50db louder, it was deafening.

One dude managed to make it on stage. As the bouncer when to tackle him, he totally booked it an dove at least 10 feet into crowd. He cleared the security line in front of the stage. There must have been about 5-6 feet between the security fence and stage, and he jumped from in front of the monitors. It was badass. The arena was seating room only, and no moshing, but after the first couple songs the first 10 rows of chairs were demolished, and pit ensued. For some reason I kept thinking Chris was singing "I'm gonna break my rusty chair".
