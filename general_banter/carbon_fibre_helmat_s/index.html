The Suomy Vandal Carbon helmet looks great but its pretty expensive.
Vemar make one in the same price range.

Bell make the M4R, I've only ever seen it on a Journo's head in a magazine (~Nov '09 AMCN Triumph rocket review).

Theres a Shark RS2R which is a nice looking helmet but I dont think it in the Oz market.

HJC Have the AC-12 (older model) the FS-10, FS-15 and HQ-1 in carbon finish.

I've got a Joe Rocket RKT-01, its exactly the same as the HJC AC-12 in construction but it has slightly different plastic spoilers and vents on it. great helmet, I've got another HJC and this one has to be almost half the weight. Worth it for the lower fatigue levels alone but it also looks neat. The only thing I dont like about it is the noise, its one of the noisier helmets with the visor opened I've ever owned. Closed its pretty average.


I havent cleaned it since an early AM ride yesterday hence the bug collection...
