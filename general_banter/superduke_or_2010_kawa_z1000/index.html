OK.. Just took the Z for a ride at the local kawa dealer... And as much i wanted to be good... it's just not... First thing I noticed was how arrow the bar are, coming from superbikes I was surprised how quickly I’ve gotten used to the wide bars..and at first the kawa seemed claustrophobic.

Other issues include:

1.) Bike feels heavy (top heavy)
2.) Really need to turn the throttle to get it to go... engine feels really tight.
3.) In the looks department the bike is really awesome looking... don't feel the bike is too plastic.. But the front does not look as good as it does in the pictures...

Over all, although I thought you guys were all crazy when you mention the soul of the duke .I'm now a believer... and as I rode out of the parking lot I just SMILED!!
