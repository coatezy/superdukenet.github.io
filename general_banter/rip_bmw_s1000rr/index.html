Well my bike is no more so im on the look out for another bike again...lol so its either a APRC aprillia rsv4 ltd edition or the new 2011 zx10r or another bimmers,any way enjoy the pics or not...lol ps i didnt walk away from this one although i did try but they kept holding me down as i had no pain anywhere,adrenalin is a awsome thing.....

broken left forearm 6 hour op to sort it with ti plates and screws
broken right wrist
fooked right knee
knocked out
whiplash
right side torn inside thigh muscle
left side torn thigh muscle
black and blue from knees to my belly button
balls the size of a pineapple
hematoma in groin the size of a cricket ball
hurt my back
dizzy spells
head aches
forgetting stuff since crash
still got my fooked up sense of humour so if any one wants a pic of my pineapple balls let me know,if i post it now it will get deleted by mods...lol


long story short over taking a jeep and trailer doing around 50-60mph nice dry day straight road nothing coming from in front or behindhe turns in to me and wiped me out,i aimed for the gap which was where the jeep decided to turn in to with no indicators no brake lights and didnt even slow down to turn in to hidden entrance..???,bikes a write off cracked frame near steering neckand im healing ok ,happened 3 weeks ago on the a470 ,got a helicopter ride and loads of morphine which was cool......

4-6 six weeks i'll be back riding so im getting either a track bike or mx bike for the winter until my new bike gets delivered in march 2011.....happy days....
