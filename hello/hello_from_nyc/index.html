Hey man, that thing steered itself to the gay bar, I just threw my hands up, went "eh... oh well!" and continued in.

If I had to make some quick comparisons:

S3: smooth as fuuuuuck engine/power delivery. About as fast as the superduke. Feels heavier. Doesn't handle as great. Brakes are kinda crappy. Much more comfortable to ride/commute on. Very controllable wheelies.

Superduke: Grunt everywhere (torque). Pulls wheelies like nobodies business, but twitchier power doesn't make it as "easy" to control. Handles excellently. feels much lighter.


Uhh... that's all I can think off the top of my head.

AGRO also had a S3 before getting his superduke. He should have some input.


When it comes down to which one I like/enjoy more, my SDR wins hands down.
