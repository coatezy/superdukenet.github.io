Hi,
After many years with japanese sportbikes, I've decided to join the Superduke family trading my R1 for a SDR 10'. Bike is definitely a blast to ride,positively surprised on how good it brakes and turns, and still getting used to the engine (fun but indeed quite different from a japanese inline four when trying to go fast on the twisties).

Bike has most of the basic goodies installed (air filter, leo vince slipons, sas removed & gear sensor disconnected, crash protectors, optional master brake cilinder, 40T rear sprocket). Just added myself the reverse gearshift kit (I'm used to having reverse shifting) and G2 0.5 cam throttle, and still in the process of installing a CPR Rottweiler airbox + new remap. (tail tidy and bar end mirrors would be nice but can wait
