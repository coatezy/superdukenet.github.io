Hi guys,
Bought a new 09 Orange and Black SD yesterday. I'll put two pics up in the right thread later.

For the Aussie members I flew up to Brisvegas from Canberra for the day to test ride 2 second hand SD's and a second hand SDR.

The privately owned SD was good. The SD at Moorooka was a bit rough and as Paul (aka looney888) mentioned, the sales people were quite forceful.

I ended up at Maroochydore (Pro Griffith Motorcycles) where the 2nd hand SDR was. Took it for a spin, and while really impressed, decided it was a bit rough for me. Then saw the new 09 on the floor. The folks at Pro Griffith were super friendly and not pushy at all. Jason there is a great guy, and did me a fantastic deal. I saved over $6,500 on RRP. And that included shipping it to my door!!!

Now just have to wait till the 19th for it to be delivered
