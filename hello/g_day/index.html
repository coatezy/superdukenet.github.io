Hi all,

Finally getting around to introducing myself... bit slack I know.

Been riding since I was a kid (40 now). A bunch of trailies, RGV250, CBR250, ZX7R, VTR1000, CBR1000, SP1, Daytona 675... to name a few. Currently ride a F700GS for work.

Picked up my '08 SD from a car dealer in Melbourne. Long story but basically a guy with more money than he know what to do with needed some garage space for a new car and couldn't be arsed selling it himself so traded it in. The car dealer had no idea what it was worth so sold it to me for $10K. It had 9K on the clock, fresh rubber and not a scratch. A bargain here in Oz.

The SD was stock when I got it. I've done most of the essentials. MH airbox, SAS removal, O2 sensor eliminated, 16T front sproket, Akra2 map and my latest purchase, some Remus hexacones. A few cosmetics including Rizoma indicators, barend mirrors, carbon heal guards and a new EvoTech tail tidy is going on this afternoon.

Hands down this is the best bike I've ever owned. I'm so in love with it. I commute, track and tour on it. Love love LOVE it!

This forum is awesome. Every mod I've made has been as a result on the threads on here. I'm a bit of a mechanical spastic but I've managed to solve every problem/query I've come across just by reading the existing posts. Thanks heaps to the Admin... awesome job.

Cheers,
Geoff
