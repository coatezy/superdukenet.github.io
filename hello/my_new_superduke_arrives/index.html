On the earlier bikes you could fit a socket in the clock surround but I'm not sure if there's anywhere to do that on the newer bikes.

it might be possible to fit one on the inner part of the tank spoiler although I can't remember exactly how much room there is between the frame spars.

If it is possible it would mean disconnecting it every time you take it off so make sure to stick it on the left one so you don't have to do it every time you want to top up your oil.
