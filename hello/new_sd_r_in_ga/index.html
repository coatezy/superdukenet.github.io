This one was shot on 180 as you leave Suches/Two Wheels Of Suches.

just a small note a buddy sent me, they are going to close 180 right at Lake Winfield Scott later this year to redo the road/dam at the lake. Supposedly it'll be closed for close to 12 months. that's going to suck, but might make the side from 129 to the lake a nice run with no traffic. lol... another FYI if you haven't been up there in a while, but Riders Hill closed. They' were the nice shop on your left as you got past Dahlonega on 60. if you need something else basic moto related, check with All Terrain Powersports near the Chick Fil A in town (behind the Slack Auto Parts store). more dual sport and off road orientated, but a good shop if you need something ordered they can get it pretty quick.

for sure we'll get together. there's not much on 60 from the rock pile to woody gap (first gap you come to as you go to Suches) so I"m sure I've ridden by there hundreds of times. Buddy of mine lives in Suches near the trout pond if you remember seeing the signs as you drop into Suches on 60.

I'll be there the weekend of the 16-18th, would love to ride with you guys. if you need anything on your way thru town or while you are up there, I've got a lot of contacts.
