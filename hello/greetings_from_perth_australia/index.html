Well I just got my open class license and went and test rode some bikes before I came across the SuperDuke.

Mate it was like a glove! perfect seat height, great position when riding oh and all that torque. Not to mention flickable and doesnt feel heavy till you try and push it around lol

Anyway I had a test ride on teh Saturday. Couldnt stop thinking about it and went in for another teast ride to make sure it wasnt just a one off feeling and laid down my deposit.

Picked up the bike on Friday after work and put 700kms on the clock in the first weekend. Very hard not to go over the 6.5k rpm mark when she just wants to keep going.

All I can say is I am in love and suprised you dont see more of them here in Perth. Saying that there are three at my work lol

Anyway just wanted to say hi and I'll see you around. Getting some Akras put on after the first service which will mos likely be monday.
