Hey,

I am just about to return to Superduke ownership after more than a year of searching for a better road bike. My verdict is that there is not one out there.
I was, I think, one of the lucky first few SD owners in the UK - I picked up my Orange SD from Powerhouse in Ashford(What happened there ? They were not THAT bad) in March 05. I had a wonderful 18 or 19 months(and about 10k miles) on her but my damn roving eye got me wondering if it was really as good as I thought.

To cut a boring story down, here I am about to p/x my current bike for a 06 Titanium bike - totally standard(one of the few, I guess), with just 2200 miles on the clock. I am kinda tempted by Gear4s' Superduke Cup bike from last season(there are so many extras I want on that bike!), but I think the standard bike makes more sense.

Anyhow, it feels good to be back, it's been a frustrating time on a variety of disappointing bikes.

Cheers !
