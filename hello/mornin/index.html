2008 Harley Davidson 883 sportster low (tallish narrowish bars, tall sissy bar, indicator/mirror/speedo relocations. Still own this one after four years, then went into dual bike ownership with a)
2002 ZX636r (owned for 9 months as something a bit more fun than the Harley, but over time started losing its appeal, especially as my local roads don't tend to be sports bike territory, so I traded it in for a bike I think (if it stops trying to die on me) will be around for quite some time as I don't see myself getting bored of it, my)
2005 KTM 990 superduke.
