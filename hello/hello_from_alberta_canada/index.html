Hello

My name is Chris, I'm 42 years old, have a three year old and another on the way so I understand...lol
I just purchased a left-over new 08 Superduke 990. I have been out of riding for the last 3 years due to some crash sustained injuries, followed by a very painful medical condition that was finally delt with two months ago.
I'm now healthy and learning to live life again!! I had talked myself out of believing I would ever ride again...just made things a little easier to live with.
Guess I was wrong, I have purchased a bike I have wanted for a long time, the SD. The trigger being an e mail from someone in my riding group with pics of his new SD!!!
I have been riding for some 30 years.I started my street riding on a 3 cyl 2 stroke Kawi and from there went directly to inline fours (GSXR'S mostly)...The SD is my first twin and I can't wait!! Looking forward to the change.
I won't be able to pick my new bike up until next week....I live in a small town 100km from where I purchased the bike. I have one last medical procedure to go through, and they say I won't be feeling to well for a few days...then back to work for 8 days (on-call,can't leave the area)...then pick bike up!!

I'm looking forward to being a part of the forum...I think I'm more of a lurker than a poster.
