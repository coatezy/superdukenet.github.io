Mick75

2011-10-10 09:33:00 UTC

Picked up a brand new white '09 SD about 3 weeks ago, 0kms straight out of the crate. Loving it, great ride, great look and great sound. looking to get some after market pipes and airbox in the future but still running her in. Looking forward to sharing my experiences with SD forum members. Been a great source of information and laughs.

Have only seen one other SD in Melbourne so far, an orange one on Dynon road near Footscray.

Been riding various bikes for quite a few years. Have owned a CBR600R (sore wrists and neck anyone?), 883 HD Sportster (nice note but quite a slug for that many cc's), KLR 650 (nice all rounder) and still have a KLR 250 for dirt squirts.

I love all bikes, even scooters have their place. Love the look and sound of harleys but posers have ruined them for me. Can appreciate jap bikes , they do what they do well. The Super Duke has a look and soul about it that just fits me perfectly. Always liked the muscle bike style, the up right seating position. I reckon the SD will be a keeper and am looking forward to clocking up many miles
