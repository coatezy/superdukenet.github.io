Hi guys,

Just thought to say hello or Shalom, as we say in Israel.

Got my 2007 SD about 3 weeks ago with 21000 Km's on it.
The bike's overall good though was standing for about two years prior to the last owner who was somewhat of misplace on the bike. It wasn't chicken strips, it was the entire hen house...

Bike was crap - great engine but everything else wrong... It wouldn't turn and when it did it was sh*t.
The front tyre was a 100 year old and cracked Pirelli which had the amount of grip you'd expect from a Durex condom.

So - got all the liquids changed, redone the front suspension and fitted a new set of Pirelli Rosso II which are great from my experience.
Suspension back to original set up and now the thing's started turning like it should!
