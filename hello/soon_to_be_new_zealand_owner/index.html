Hi Guys,

I ordered a new antrcite (best get that out the way first as I'm sure I'm in for some abuse) SD yesterday. It should be ready for pickup on Friday, so the countdown has begun.

I tested a few other bikes such as the Aprilia Tuono, Triumph Daytona 675 etc. but none left me with the ear to ear grin that the SD did.

The Aprilia was good and I could have lived with it, but after riding the SD, the Aprilia felt almost vague in the corners in comparison although it certainly has some nice grunt. The Daytona was also a nice bike but was agony on my wrists.

In the end there was really no contest and I'm just couting the days, hours, minutes and seconds until it's ready for pickup.
