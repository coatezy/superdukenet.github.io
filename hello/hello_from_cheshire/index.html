TimR

Hi All, my name is John from Cheshire.
Not bought a Super Duke yet but I nearly did last week off Ebay (Premier bikes bought it in the end - it was a minter). I am going to wait until next year as I tend to keep my bikes off the road during winter (wimp I know!!) - I'm lucky enough to own 9 bikes inc 2 x KTM's - a 990 Adventure and a 400EXC. I am also very lucky that my missus is nuts about bikes, she has a Honda XBR500, Yamaha 225 Serow off road and a Moto Guzzi V7 cafe racer.

I am mid 50's now & although I have been riding since I was 12 I'm afraid I struggle on the race type bikes I have. I have a Buell XB12s which I guess "style wise" is the closest thing I have to a SD and out of all the bikes we have, I love riding this the most so I can only imagine what a SD will be like.

This year we took the KTM 990 to Switzerland and Italy to ride all the big mountain passes and I met quite a few Super Duke riders. Just fell in love with the SD and I hope to get one in early 2014.

This is a great forum and already I have read lots of interesting articles, tips and tricks etc

thanks and regards
John
