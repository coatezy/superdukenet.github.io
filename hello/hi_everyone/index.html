Get a bottle of wine, or a spliff, (or whatever your tipple is), and have a retrospective read through the threads. It's years worth of SD owners going through the trials and experience of owning this fantastic bike. You're in good company mate. And the more you read, the more you'll see that that the SD is a great bike - but has some inherent flaws that can be worked around and solved. All quite easily. But you will be asking the same questions that countless SD owners have asked before.

So the more you read up front - them more you'll get an idea of the things that you'll be wanting to know. And most of the answers are already out there! But feel free to ask because there will always be answers here.

Seems to me - and I've owned a lot of bikes and known a lot of bike owners - but the SD seems to attract a better sort of person, more knowledgeable, more sorted, more able to exchange views, more approachable. Just go and check out a GSX forum and be dismayed at the wanker/idiot type that frequent those places.

Enjoy the bike. You made the right decision.

PS, I used to live in Bedminster. Miss those easy routes out to good roads - especially Wales. Stuck in the smog here. Traffic light hell.
