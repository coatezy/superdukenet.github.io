2013's Race Bike done

Viking

2012-12-19 09:58:00 UTC

Bike is almost finished..........

I acheived my weight goals for the start of my 2013 season.

Final weight in=
All fluids on max ready to go, no fuel= 158.4kg (349.2lbs)
As above, inc 10L fuel on grid ready to race= 167.2kg (368.9lbs)
10L fuel weighs approx 9kg.


A big change in design from 2012's Echo livery. I chose this myself as i like it to be subtle with a few trick touches. Went with a silver metal flake as a base in various quatities on black/silver, then finished with a holograghic effect flake to make it flip and sparkle in the sun. My idea was that it will look very plain and subdued but as it pans past the TV cameras in the sun the flake effect will make the bike look alot brighter than it is.








I tried to show the effect in the painwork in this shot but the picture does it no justice as it's too dull outside at this time of year.
Boogies Bike Paint put in a fantastic effort. The paint job is like glass!
LR Designs had a free reign for graphics and did very well in coming up with the effect that fits well with the bikes overall look.
