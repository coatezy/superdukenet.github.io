I loved my 916, 4 years and 40K miles later I still loved it but moved on. I wished I'd kept it.

I have to admit, the superduke is the only bike I've loved the same.

When I had my 05 SD, a friend who owns a bike shop asked me what I would replace the SD. I said I was happy and at that stage had no plans to change bike. He went on to say, if it was written off and I had no choice, what would I get.

I said I get another identical bike, which was exactly what I did when it was stolen.

I have never owned identical bikes back to back, I think that says a lot about my SD love!

Went out for a quick ride on the weekend and meet some nice South African people at Mt Mee.
They are hardcore bike fans and wanted to know about my bike and when we got talking I realised the guy wanted to get something like the KTM 690 enduro, much like I myself have considered.
He said to me "so why not trade it in on the enduro?" and I told him - "I know I should cos I would have my license for longer, it would be cheaper to run and I could also play in the dirt - but I just can't sell this bike. Its just too much fun even to commute on."
It has its quirks, but quite simply this is an awesome bike no matter how you look at it.
