I dont know why they are slower but they do seem to be. I know a few of the early race bikes had bottom end failures so it is likely they are detuned to prevent this.

The place that dyno'ed my 07 SDR said they think the rev limiter cuts in too early. With PC111 my 07 SDR is only making 112 bhp (up from 109bhp)

All I know is that Klink and TJ on early bikes were leaving me for dead at Cadwell and I had throttle on stop, Power Commander and Quickshifter to help.

The geometry seems softer too. I have just spent 300 plus quid on front end but it still doesnt give me the same confidence as my 05 did.

The bigger tank is a bonus for road riding but otherwise I wish I had kept my 05 for the track.
