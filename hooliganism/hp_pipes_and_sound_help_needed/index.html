HP pipes and sound. Help needed.

MarkyMark76

2007-10-19 00:36:00 UTC

Hi friends,

I just my new HP mufflers and installed them on my SD. I got the 300mm model with SPS Corse end caps. They are the best looking pipes for the SD bare non IMHO. They look awesome and fit very well with the bike's lokks. They were also easy to install and fit very well.

I'm still running with the stock maping. Will get the Akra soon. On my first ride with these pipes I got a second gear wheelie on gas only which is a first for me and this bike. Maybe it was the cold air...

I got them with what is supposed to be a new DB killer.

Problem is I don't like the sound. Without the DB killers they sound awesome but man are they ever loud? They almost hurt your ears with the engine just idling.

With the DB killers in the sound level is better but they sound aweful. It's like they take out all the low frequencies and let the highs through. The result is a high pitched sound that's so bad that I think it's the worst sound I have ever heard coming from a V twin.

When riding around town I short shift as much as possible to keep the revs and the sound level as low as possible. Not because it's loud but because it sounds ridiculous.

I was wondering, does anybody else own these pipes and could you share your experience with me?

I suppose my only way out is to either sell the pipes or try to come up with a modified set of DB killers that would sound better without beeing too loud.

Maybe the previous version of the DB killers weren't so bad, I don't know.

Can anybody help me with this?

Thanks in advance!
