Bermo KTM

2011-05-16 03:51:00 UTC

Sticky backed hook and loop, carpet portion on key both sides. Sticky back hook piece on under side of tank panel. I leave my key on the bike at all times and go sans key loss wherever I go. I have done this on all my bikes and have traveled many miles and many days away from home with no key issues ever. I have had a bike that i disconnected the kick stand switch and used it as a safety no start when away from the bike with a month of touring Mexico and no issues there either. Keychains are for cages. The less clutter the better.
