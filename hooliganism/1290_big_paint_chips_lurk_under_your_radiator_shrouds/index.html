Also sorry to stray off topic but it's worth mentioning to save you heartache down the track. Add some off these little grip pads used for furniture to the raised sections of your tank where the side fairings sit before they eat through the paint completely. I grabbed these ones from Bunnings for about $2.

There's also a few places where you should be (if you're worried about rubbing etc) adding 3M ventureshield to the frame. Most notably is the clips of the radiator over flow bottle and opposing side cover and I also added "tabs" to the frame where my boots will rub.

I plan to add more 3M to the top of the tank where the fairing sits & the headlight too as I like playing cat & mouse with mates and I'm sick of getting stone chips!
