Well Zen, I've been thinking about this one and I think this issue works on two levels.
Logically you're completely, unarguably correct in everything you say, these guys are going to give us a bad name with Joe Citizen, they are pushing the risk envelope too far and sooner or later either they or Joe Citizen are gonna end up dead....
BUT...Emotionally the yahoo in me says, go guys go! I wish I could hoon like that, scythe through traffic, pull wheelies at will and generally give the finger to the man. My inner 6 year old knows that this kind of behaviour is big and clever...albeit a bit life threatening, but there's the thrill.
So what do I think? Is this kind of carry on crass or clever?
Unfortunately it's both. So...I tend to keep my hooning for quiet back roads where the chances of ghastly collision are slightly reduced, but I still think that vid looks big and clever...
