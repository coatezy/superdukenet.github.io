Just a point of clarification: It's the starter motor torque limiter - nothing to do with the clutch although it is a sort of starter motor clutch. Lots of people have had them replaced under warranty and a few have had them replaced at no cost out of warranty. Irrespective, get it done soon as it's a destructive thing that will cost heaps if you don't.
