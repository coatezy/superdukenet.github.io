bic_bicknell

2010-04-20 21:22:00 UTC

Ok, second submission.

Approaching traffic lights, filtering through stationary traffic towards the front of the cue. Three cars from the front the passenger door of a car on the right opened up four foot in front of me. Nothing I could do.

Two fingers on the brake helped not one bit. Anchored on but no good. In slow motion the door opened, the bike sailed on, the door edge connected with my foot and crushed it into the bike. The door continued to open, the bike continued onwards and my foot got guillotined between the two.

From 20mph to nothing in about half a second. There was a dull thud and me screaming with pain. But no bones broken Just a lot of pain for a few weeks.

So actually a good advert for Alpine star SMX boots.

Bike was a VFR Honda and it sustained some weird damage as it got smashed into the car on my left, it never rode straight again. But then it got stolen two weeks later so I never had to fix it. Hated that bike anyway, good engine, but hated the linked brakes, hated the soft suspension, hated other Honda riders nodding at me.

Got paid out and bought myself a Superduke!!!:lol:

So really, this is what got me to where I am.
