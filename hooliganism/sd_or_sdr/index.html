joe thinks we mainly ride the same roads 98% of the time and it really depends on your weight and your roads as much as anything else said in the above.

Here's my thinking, ... If I were 160lbs and had these roads I frequent, which are very rough...

the SDR would beat you to death unless you reworked the suspension for the task at hand.

I am 235 lbs and the SD's suspension and ride for me suits my style, I rarely do track days,

I ride 60+ miles to reach the twisty's and have a rough racetrack and public roads... I also want a new SDR but I know if I do get one to replace the 07 SD,

I will have to soften the seat and suspension to suit my taste, the view from the cockpit... from the pilots perspective on either is the same.

For me, it is how I feel after an all-day ride, if my neck hurts (like on a typical pretzel bike).... or my ass hurts for a day later, this won't work for me.
