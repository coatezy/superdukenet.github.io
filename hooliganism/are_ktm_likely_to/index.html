I got a few clues from one of KTMS top mechanics that the new SD is due out on 2013, but they'd love to get it out earlier in 2012.

They will probably be a bigger engine, more power, etc, but I learned the most from what they wouldn't tell me.

What is clear, is that they realise how importnat the Superduke is to the KTM brand, and that they are well overdue improving this bike to stay on track with the competition, simply because hey are swamped with development work on the rest of the range.

I'd guess we'll see a detuned RC8 engine, with an underslung zorst, tail end like an RC8, and none of the scary bikini fairing stuff we've seen from the venom designs. They seem to know that's NOT what we want.


Does the SD need more power? Not for me. The 130 RWBHP is plenty on a naked. I'd prefer to see smoother fuelling, a prettier rear end, low zorst and slipper clutch as standard, with a suble evolution of the current iconic design - and those lovely wheels from the SMR.
