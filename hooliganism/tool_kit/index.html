Don't forget the KTM pen.

I will never forget sitting around for hours in the dealer’s showroom while they prepared my new 2007 SD...I had nothing to do. It was too far out of town to go do anything else...and after looking at every bike in the showroom several times, (most were American built Victory models..In which I have zero interest)..so I was just sitting staring around. Then the salesman came over and handed me a KTM briefcase (the tools were inside) and then ceremoniously handed me the KTM pen...he seemed very pleased with that pen...It was as if he was saying, "Here it is...your new $16,000 dollar KTM pen". I looked at the pen for a while, wondering why anyone would reinvent a ballpoint pen that takes two hands to operate...then I dumped the briefcase of tools onto his desk and spent the rest of the time looking them over...good tools and although I have plenty of other tools that will do the job I usually use at least some of the KTM tool kit when working on the SD. I have added some tools to the kit including a miniature set of vice grips …all fit under the seat where the canister used to be…and I am now on my second KTM pen...
