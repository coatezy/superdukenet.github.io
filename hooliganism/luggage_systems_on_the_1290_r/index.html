2016-03-13 17:21:00 UTC

You are correct the KTM powerparts bags won't work with Akrapovic full exhaust. Have you done any modifications to the tail of your bike? (Like a tail tidy). If so, you may have issues like I am with the luggage displacing the rear turn signals.

One of the best looking set ups that I have seen was the Wolfman BetaPlus strapped to the passenger seat. Here's a link to the post/pic of it, its probably about 15 posts down, by user REDTALE2000: viewtopic.php?f=1&t=24511

As far as the Blaze bags, I would contact Revzilla and ask when it will be available. They'll email you when they come in. Last time I did that for a pair of gloves they gave me free overnight shipping once they came in.

Hey- I don't know, I guess it depends on which tail tidy you have done. Here's a video link that clearly shows the bags fitted to a bike WITHOUT the full akra. Judge for yourself whether you think your turn signals will clear it. The video is in German, so I don't know what he's saying, but it provides a good shot of the bags from side and rear:
https://youtu.be/SdYnd9O_dlk?t=586

I would try emailing the manufacturer of the bags and see if they know whether it can be fitted to a bike with the Akrapovic exhaust.

Otherwise, I'd really consider the Wolfman bag, and possibly reinstalling OEM tail parts. The tail tidys leave you hamstrung when it comes to fitting luggage. I bought a bungee net for simple luggage solution and found that with a tail tidy installed there is virtually nowhere to anchor the hooks. It sucks.
