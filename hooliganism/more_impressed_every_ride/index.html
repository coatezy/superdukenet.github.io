Everytime I take this bike out for a blast I come back more impressed than when I left. First bike I've ever experienced this with.

I've got over 12K miles and love riding this bike as much (or more) now than when I bought it almost three years ago. Track or street, no bike has been as rerwarding (and I've owned more than my fair share of bikes in my 46 years). This is a good thing as I plan on buying the new 1200 SD, but only after they have a year or so on them to work out most of the kinks.

I'll have no problem sticking with this bike for a couple more years! My buddy Dino has 53,000 miles on his 07 and like myself, rides the shite out of it so longevity shouldn't be an issue. Fingers crossed.
