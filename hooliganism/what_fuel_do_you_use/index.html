2008-06-17 01:25:00 UTC

RON is a universal rating - but it can be artificially increased/modified using additives.
For example adding Ethanol increases the RON rating of std 91 RON fuel (to 93 I think), but in a wicked little twist actually decreases the fuels efficiency.
Now theoretically the higher the RON the more efficient the fuel is but with Ethanol you the worst of everything - decreased mileage, decreased efficiency and decrease in engine life.
