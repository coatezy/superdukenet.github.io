ah, the memories...

back when i used to race enduros on a 620 EXC ('95-'96) the bike would come through the front door and into a corner of the living room (i was living in a very small studio back then, my fathers old office...) .

this was done every day, but the real nightmare was after sunday races/practice...

clothes were in boxes and the closets were the tool shed... with nails driven into the doors to hold up T wrenches... the kitchen cupboards had coffee, sugar, air filter oil, engine oil and brake fluid... the kitchen sink was used to wash out the air filter...

needless to say that when i left that home my father was not happy with the state i left it in
