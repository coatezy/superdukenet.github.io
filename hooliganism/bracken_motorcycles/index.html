get a quote first. I had my 05 SD serviced at Bracken and havent used them since.

It was about 450.00 quid as I recall and they kept the bike for a week as they couldnt / didnt bother to get a new exhaust gasket any sooner.

I had little faith in them as the kids I saw in the workshop were about 16 and had the intelligence of plankton.

In fact it is hard to see how the business failed as they charged top top top dollar surely couldnt have paid much rent for their shanty town premises and certainly couldnt have paid their oompa loompa technicians much.

Whilst I personally wouldnt use Bracken for servicing again there is no justice in the world if they go under and Laguna continue to operate. RIP Bracken, best of luck Paul in your new venture but for christ sake, make those kids dress and act like responsible adults as we are trusting our pride and joys to them.
