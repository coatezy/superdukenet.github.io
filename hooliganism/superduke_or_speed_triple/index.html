2009-09-04 11:01:00 UTC

Just got my SD last Saturday - I test rode Speed Triple, Ducati 1100, and Ducati 1100s a couple days before I got the Super Duke. To me, the Speed triple seemed somewhat "tame" in handling and engine - liked the bike a lot and certainly could live with one, but I had remembered my SD test ride from 1.5 years back and decided the Duke was the pick. The Ducati 1100 was not very impressive to me, however, the Ducati 1100s was a very close 2nd for me compared to the Duke - 1100s just seemed a little less kick and handling.

When I originally tested the Duke, I came back to the dealer that sells Honda Yamaha Suzuki KTM and at the time I owned FZ1 and Suzuki Vstrom DL1000 - for me, I told them, the SD was the FZ1 handling with the Vstrom engine, which is why I got the SD - I always loved the FZ1 handling and the low end grunt of the DL1000 - now I have both in one bike! Whoopie!
