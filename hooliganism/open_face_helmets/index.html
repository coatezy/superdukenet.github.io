robinpeck

2009-02-18 21:20:00 UTC

My main helmet is a full face Shoei, but I would also like to have a more casual "summertime" helmet. But I'd like a proper open face helmet, not one of those "cruiser" hats. I am wondering if anyone with experience can recommend a brand. I understand that Davida is good (but here in Canada they are both very expensive and very difficult to obtain) and I am also looking at one of the Noland helmets (open face but with clear faceshield and a dark shield that comes down over this)..there are also some very reasonably priced Chinese clones of the Noland but ..but since "made in China" now means "don't put it in your mouth", I wonder about the wisdom of entrusting my head to that cost cutting economy. Any experienced advice will be appreciated.
