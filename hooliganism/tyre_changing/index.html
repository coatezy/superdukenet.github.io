DribbleDuke

2012-01-07 19:35:00 UTC

This time of year does make it very hard compared to warmer temps.

Youl need 2 sets of rim protectors for a start, 2 tyre irons is plenty, i use wax free spray polish such as Mr Sheen on the rim of the tyre/wheel to make fitting a slippier easier affair!

The trick is when you have the lower half of the tyre over the rim, kneel down on the tyre thereby squashing it so it slots into the centre of the rim (if you look at the profile of a rim the centre is lower than the sides, when the tyre sits in this centre it gives the tyre those few extra mm`s to enable the top to go over. If you do it right you dont need the tyre levers for the top part of the rim
