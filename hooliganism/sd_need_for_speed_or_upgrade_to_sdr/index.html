+1 on Ducati Streetfighter. The one I saw on track was leaving my SDR for dead everywhere.

If you are trying to get more than about 5 - 10 reliable bhp out of a Superduke you are wasting your money.

The 07 SDR has no engine mods and is, if anything slower than my 05 SD. The 08 / 09 SDR has a claimed 10bhp increase but all at the top end and there are lots of stories of build quality issues with these.

If you add up the cost of all the mods you have listed plus what your bike is worth You wont be far short of the price of a used Streetfighter either.
