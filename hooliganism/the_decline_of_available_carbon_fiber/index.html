bic_bicknell

2011-04-16 13:12:00 UTC

Just grabbed a few hours to fit the new CF headlight cowls and fly screen.

They are good carbon and resin quality and the fit is reasonable bar a few frustrating points. The fly screen fits perfectly. The two headlight cowls both have the same issues. The mounting points for the attachment screws are in the correct position but they are fixed to the inside of the CF panels with a hand applied fillet of resin. This has been over applied so that the diameter of the tube is too big, in places, for them to fit into the corresponding sunken holes in the headlight shell halves. Ten minutes of finishing with a file sorts it out but it's not what you'd expect from an after market part.

But the worst bit is that the holes in all five mounting points are too big for the large self tapping screws. The way I remedied this was to fill them all with chemical metal and before it set hard I screwed in all the screws after coating the threads with Vaseline. Sorted it all out but added another half hour to what should have been a quick 20 minute fitting job.

Anyway, completed mask looks good for all you Carbon lovers out there. They also do a complete one-piece carbon cowl for the '07 onwards model if you fancy that option rather than replacing the panels separately. Not sure how all the mounting points and hinge points will be on this option though.
