If you are in the States, the Yoydyne is like the Sigma although I have been recently informed that they are not the same.
The KTM/Suter is tits and I loved mine much better than the Yoyodyne.
I am suggesting here that you scour the internet and possibly wait for one to appear on eBay.
You should be able to get one for $800 or less. The dealers sell them for $1500 I think.
Still worth the extra $500 or so over the Yoyodyne, IMO.

On the subject of wear. My clutch pack barely wore at all in two years but the pressure plate and other hard parts of the Suter/KTM wore down in less than one season.
The Yoyodyne was taken out after 5 race weekends or so and looked brand new still.
