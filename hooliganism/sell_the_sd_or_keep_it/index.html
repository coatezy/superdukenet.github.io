I haven't had my SD long but my other ride is a K4 GSXR 750 which I've had from new and I love the SD I think it's such a blast to ride, I've done overtakes in places on the country lanes I travel to work that I've never done on the GSXR because I'm not sure I could get round the tight bend at the other end on it but on the SD I didn't even think about it that much.

Obviously everybody likes different things about bikes otherwise we'd all be riding the same bike so it's hard to say what is right for you, personally I'm really happy with the SD it's more comfortable on the A/B roads to work and through the traffic and I love the mid range grunt but I also have the Gixer if I fancy something different or need to do some motorway miles.

Also depends what kind of riding you do too, if you rarely get a chance to ride the twisty backroads and spend most of your time on mainly straight roads you probably won't be getting the benefit out of the SD.
