Here you go. You can see that it fits quite well but bleeds a bit of light round the edges. I can't remember what fittings it came with but I now have three round velcros permanently attached to the headlight so I can pull it on and off easily. If you get one I'd put a bit of rubber or felt on the back of the cover because vibration will rub a mark on the center of your plastic headlight. Too late for me, the damage is done but you could save yourself the bother.
