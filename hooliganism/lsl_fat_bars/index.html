bic_bicknell

2010-12-09 03:16:00 UTC

I just finished the install on these, haven't ridden it yet but greatly prefer the lower grips.

got them in the US via Motostrano.com (from speiglerusa.com 's dealer list)
good guys, I'd buy from them again.

I also cut 39mm off each end, it works out to ~6mm wider than OEM.
Setting the steering stops at 25mm long (from the aluminium to the bolt top), makes it so the switch pods and throttle cables will clear the tank. There'll be ~5mm clearance, with the switches mounted plumb, and the throttle cables angled forward at 4 o'clock.

I'm also running the bars rolled forward slightly, at the "+2" mark. They can be rolled back, but they'll be less tank clearance unless you increase the steering stop length. The levers are about 20mm below level, so if you like the levers at a steep down angle you'll need to do some adjusting on the brake line routing. I had to move the banjo fitting forward a bit to get the line routed more forward on the fork tube.

hope this helps
