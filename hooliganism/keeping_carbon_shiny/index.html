Remember where you got this tip:

Lemon Pledge wipes.


Yes, Lemon Pledge wipes.





Use them on your helmet & shield, carbon bits, windshield, anything that you can't/don't wash & wax. The wax in the wipes fills in minor scratches in the clear coat/plastic, and helps objects slide off instead of stick (like bugs off your helmet). Minimizes fog build-up on the inside of your face shield. Smells nice, too. Clean your gauge viewscreen, headlight, anything clear or tinted plastic. Doesn't work on the matte finish of the SD, you want to use it mainly on shiny surfaces. Take a small ziplock bag with 1 or 2 wipes, and a micorfiber cloth (to polish it off) on your rides. They last quite while, so don't throw it away after every use. OK, now you owe me one.



Do not try this with the Orange Pledge wipes - even though it works the same; the scent can be overpowering inside a helmet, and the orange attracts bees. Trust me on this one.
