Hotbrakes

2012-06-21 14:58:00 UTC

You're just mounting it wrong, if you thread the rear strap behind the tank pivot bolts the buckle is out of the way and the seat mounts without any problem. The base looks decent on the bike, and the zip off bag allows easy access to the gas cap. Plus it says KTM all over it, looks cool, and fits a ton of stuff.

But, if it's not suiting your needs, check out twistedthrottle.com, they have a ton of bag options including the type that mount to a ring fixed to the gas cap bolts.
