Hey mate, looks like a few nice days planned. On day one I recommend not taking Lions Rd. This is a narrow dangerous rough road with a large area of unfenced road. Cattle on the road. The buggers are not afraid of vehicles and will not move out of the way. Much better to stay on the Mt Lindsay Hwy through Rathdowney and the run up through the hills after Palen Creek is a blast on the SD. You can pick up the Summerland Way about 5 klicks after you cross the border. This is a good fast run to Kyogle.

On day 2 the run up the Bruxner from Casino to Tenterfield is my alltime fav. Don't forget to stop at the Drake pub for a cool drink and check out the bikes set up in the bar. A mint CB1100R and lots of motoracing memorabillia around the walls. And after the pub - 30 klms of twisties - bloody brilliant.

I'm with Reb, Bruxner is great. Rathdowney, Kyogle, Murwillumbah is one of my all time favorite loops from the gold coast.
I kind of like Lions Rd, there are some unpleasant bits but its worth the run for a handful of excellent corners and the nice scenery. Its also a good switchback/sub-loop if you want to end up in Kyogle to go Rathdowney/My Lindsay, summerland way, grevillia then north up Lions Rd and back down My Lindsay again - because its even more enjoyable the second time round on the same day.

As for Day 3, I'd do option B, Numinbah, advancetown, beechmont is great. Also worthy of a detour (about 10Kms) is pine creek rd that comes off numinbah valley rd going to springbrook/mudgeeraba, Just go up to the top of the hill and come back down, or continue on down the other side on the mudgee/springbrook rd (but then you'd miss the advancetown raceway..).

O'rileys is tiring but awesome, you will be fatigued when you get back to Canungra, allow time for a coffee/break. Look out for tourist coaches. I've done that road a hundred times and never seen a cop out there, nail it.

Either way, it will be a good run, perfect SD territory all round. Play safe and have fun.
