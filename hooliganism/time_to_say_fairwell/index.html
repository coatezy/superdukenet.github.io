Time to say fairwell

jambox

2018-10-08 19:51:00 UTC

So after almost 10 years of ownership, I will be parting ways with my SD next weekend.

There are a few reasons for selling up, main one being a newborn coming along and me having no restraint to pinning it when in the twisties (I live in the start of the Scottish Highlands, so...). That and the fact that I've not really been using it much over the last few years and it deserves to be used (and abused).

I have loved owning the beast and have many fond memories, but am happy to move on.

There will be a few things going up for sale, off the top of my head a Rottweiler box, LSL steering damper, Motohooligan tank brackets and rearset adjusters among other stuff that I've acquired over the years.

This is truly one of the most involving forums that I have come across with some brilliant members and I will stop by every now and then.

As they say "Thanks for the memories and toodle pip!"

Jambox.
