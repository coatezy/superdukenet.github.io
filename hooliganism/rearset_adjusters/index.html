If you're trying to get your knee down, and you're leaning the bike over to the edge of the tyres, and, you're having your bum halfway off the seat... then...

... you're most likely pivoting around the tank and sticking your knee forward, rather than outwards. When starting out it does feel a bit funny to stick your knee out, the natural thing to do is to hang off and pivot around the tank sticking your knee out forward. If you can mount your bike steadily (I've used an Abba Superbike Stand before) you can practise in the garage.

Also make sure your inner foot is tippy-toeing and angled 90 degrees out on the peg instead of being "flat" on the peg.

Different pegs might help, but they're not the critical ingredient in getting your knee down.
