It all has to do with eighths. The 5 in 520 is the space between the pins, center to center in eighths. The 2 in 520 is the space from side to side of the links. A 530 chain has 3/8" space between. A 525 is 5/8" pin to pin and 2.5 eighths side to side, or 5/16". All about surface area on load bearing.
