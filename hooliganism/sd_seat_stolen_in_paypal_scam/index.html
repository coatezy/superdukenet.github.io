1. never sell outside your country
2. if buying outside your country, make sure you pay with Paypal (so you can dispute the item if it is junk/never arrives)
3. ALWAYS USE "TRACKED SHIPPING", and get a delivery receipt (this is how you prove to ebay/paypal you delivered). BTW, ebay OWNS paypal.
