2012-04-14 15:19:00 UTC

Here is another option to think / know about. The stock cans from the KTM Adventure SE and SM bikes are sitting in your brothers / cousins shed collecting spiders and dust. They also install aftermarket cans on thier bikes. Unlike the SD they are two seperate cans / with cats, that can be de-cated, and de piped. I cut one apart a few weeks ago and removed every thing from the inside. My plan is to find some perforated pipe and wrap exhaust fibers around it as you do on two smoke dirt bikes. I will cut the can near the inlet and yank the guts out, all of it. The plane is to make a sleave at this junction and pop rivet the pices together for future replacing. The trick is to insert the perf wraped pipe inside so it does not rattle. It might get welded to the inlet side and jamed to meet the outlet.
You can run one or both cans with a little head scratching.

This will look stock to the cops, but should really flow and go.

PS. Got a spare set of stock cans in the shed with me new to me hand me down 04 950Adventure, cause the Adventure has the AKO,s already installed.
