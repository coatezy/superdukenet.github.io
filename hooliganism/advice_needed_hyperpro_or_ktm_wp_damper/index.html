I've had the white power KTM damper and it was good - but no where near as good as the Scotts damper that I've had on for a few years now. The Scotts is the most sensitive adjustment I've ever experienced and it's better for adjusting on the move. It's so easy to back it off when you come into a town or traffic and then dial it up for the bumpies and fast roads. They are a lot more cash than a telescopic damper but seriously, they are better. I got mine from DRSWade's race bike and it's one of the best bit of kit I've put on. Look out for a used on on EBay.
