2011-04-29 12:38:00 UTC

Open the pdf, go to the correct page.
Zoom in to a good sharp image size.
Take a screenshot
Save the screenshot in tiff or bmp (any lossless format)
Open inkscape
Import your image
Find "Trace bitmap" in the menu
Fiddle with the settings till you get good results (or search google for a tutorial)
TADA! Logo in vector format.
