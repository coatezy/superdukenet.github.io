yeah, i feel like that often, i miss the near-300 km/h runs.....

i think it is largely a case of "grass is greener on the other side"... when i had my 1000rr, i complained that i need a bike like SD... now that i have one, i complain that i need a superbike.... in truth i need 3 bikes: SD, superbike (new CBR1000rr or gsxr1000) and a dirt bike... if i could i'd add a super-tourer to that (for those loooong trips across the country), and busa or zx14, or k1200s for the days i just feel like speeding...

SD is a very versatile machine, it does many things well, great for race track/street/hooliganism, ok for (slow) touring, it even does well of road (ok, gravel road)... it handles amasing, is quite comfy, and the power isn't bad either. only aspect where she's lacking, is high speed... over 200km/h you can really feel that you could use few more ponnies, some more wind protection, and more confident handling...

what can you do? can't have it all.... at least not all in one.
