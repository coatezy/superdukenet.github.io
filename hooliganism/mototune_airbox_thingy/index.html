There are not only benefits with the Motobox.

A couple of weeks ago I went to a local track. I didn't have my dB-killers, so I never gave full throttle. Even so I was pulled over and told to take it easy with the throttle since they'd clocked me for 99 dB. I was suggested to put the dB-killers in to get below the required 95 dB.
Last Sunday I had the dB-killers in and really got into it with full throttle where I could. This time I was black flagged, and they'd clocked me at 102.8 dB - that was with dB-killers!
So, no more full throttle that day...the sound of the Motobox is raw and beautiful but it's also very very loud!
