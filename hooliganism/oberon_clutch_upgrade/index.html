bic_bicknell

2011-04-14 13:41:00 UTC

Davey,

If your clutch is working fine, then in my experience it is not an upgrade. If your clutch slave is getting tired, then most feel this is a better route than the standard KTM slave. As for the base plate, heat is the enemy of the plastic base plate, the off road guys tend to suffer from base plate distortion and the Oberon aluminium base plate solves the heat distortion issue.

Having said that, if you can afford the plate as well as the slave it may be an idea to replace both at the same time. The hole in the middle of my original base plate had become distorted. A quick bit of minimal filing sorted this, until the Oberon base plate arrived.
