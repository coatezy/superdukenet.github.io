We DON'T on-sell personal details

jmann

2016-06-23 00:41:00 UTC

Comrades: One of the perennial problems we have with new members and registration is that they are reticent to provide personal details because they think we might nick their bike or on-sell their details for profit. As all of you should know, we don't do that and thus nobody should have received spam Etc. as a consequence of providing their details at registration. We only use these details to try to avoid giving membership to the types of people that the potential new members are worried about. Indeed, I often ring people (from Australia at my own expense) and if they don't pick up or something looks fishy then I don't register them.

It would be useful if some of our existing members could respond to this post and just confirm that Superduke.Net does not engage in on-selling so that guest users will see the post before registration. A response from both long-termers and new members would be equally useful. Just say something like "It's true".
