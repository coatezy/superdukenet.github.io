Hey Guy I used to own a 08 speedy and to be honest I didn't like it much the front end was not confidence inspiring and I just couldn't get it to handle like it should have. Also the centre of gravity is to high and it feels top heavy.
I liked the engine it had good power down low and mid range but ran out of puff quickly top end.
The dash liked to get water in it if it rained the paint was really brittle it was so bad that I had paint coming off the side covers the first time I washed it lol. It liked to jump out of gear at times it also had a bad vibe from the wheels at about 180kph that I didn't get looked at because I had had enough of the bike by then. The front end would dive really bad under hard breaking at speed and the rear shock started to get corrosion on it early. The engine would run hot so I had to flush the cooling system to get all the sand out and I put 25% coolant and 1/2 bottle water wetter in it and it did make a bit of difference bit I still think the rad is to small. Now I bought this bike brand new and sold it within 3 months. All in all even the controls look sort of on the cheap side but they are a cool looking bike.
If it was me I would get the Street Triple R they go really good and handle really good as well smaller and lighter. But I'm 5'6" so it probably would suit me better.
