Perelli Rosso Corsa

shadowman

2011-02-14 10:23:00 UTC

I know there has been endless tyre chat on the forum over the years but I can’t see any mention of the Rosso Corsas.

My rear was destroyed in some high speed stupidity at the end of last riding season after which hibernation followed. I'm just getting sorted for the coming year now.

I have ridden on Pirelli Corsa 111 for as long as I can remember for fast road / track work. I don't do any track stuff on the SD but also I don’t rack up many miles on the road either so my preference is still for good feel and grip over durability.

The Corsa 111 seem to have been replaced by the Rosso Corsa now and I just wondered if anybody here had used them on the SD and what their impressions were. Looking at what has been written before many people seem happy with M5's or 2CT's but tyres are as much in the mind as anything else (all the top sports tyres can do lap times few of us can aspire to) and so I'm inclined to stick with Pirelli unless the forum experience suggests it's a bad idea.

Any / all comments / advice welcome.

Don't compare the OEM corsa III with the Diablo Rosso Corsa, it is a totally different tyre. I have seen the C III on other bikes and the are not very good. Regarding the warm up on the DRC's, in NZ the temp is not very hot and in winter I run a bit less pressure, never had any problems and the tyres grip pretty well from cold. I look forward having a spin in Townsville as soon as I get the bike over, I can only imagin how they would grip in 30C temps.
