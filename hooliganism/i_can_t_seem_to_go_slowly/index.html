I can't seem to go slowly?

Dutch

2011-07-11 09:19:00 UTC

I've been gaylording around for a few weeks on my "summer cruiser" - a Ducati Sport Classic 1000. It's been fun, and I've enjoyed gurgling around in jeans and trainers with a half face lid on, and even surprising a few people with it's handling and midrange - but - it was time to get the proper "duke" out again, so this week I've been back to black and orange... and OMFG.

In just two short weeks I had literally forgotten how bonkers the Superduke R is. It won't let me go slowly. I can't help racing everyone, chucking it on it's ear at every opportunity, and coasting to a gentle stop just isn't in this bike's DNA. How the fook did I keep a clean licenense so long! Why are these bikes allowed on the road? I'm sending cars and bicycles scattering ahead of me at the bark of the open Akras - which is very helpful for filtering through Camden. (...it's a safety accessory, Officer)

If you know anyone who was on the road in Islington, Camden or the West End between 8.45 and 9.15 this morning (especillay on a bicycle) please pass on my sincere apologies. Sorry.
