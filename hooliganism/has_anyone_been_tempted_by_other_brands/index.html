On another front altogether. I am tempted, for another but not as a replacement, a Triumph Scrambler. I would want a 06 or 07 as they were carborated. I am thinking of adding into a stall a bike that is new running but still holds all the virtues of a simpler time. Air cooled, cable clutch, spoked wheels, carbs, heck I would even like a kick starter. I want the quinticential motorcycle, one with little pretence or one that does not expect so much from me. When I ride the SD, I feel it my duty to treat it with the wild abandon she so deserves. I am not always up to the task, and therefore need to compensate for times that I forgot to wheelie past the SUV passing in a no passing zone area and past every kid on a bike smoking the rear tyre away with a rolling burnout. The best way that I have come up with to try to make it up to her is to wheelie past the Ghost Riders biker bar on Sunday afternoons right smack downtown in my little one horse town. She absolutly is thrilled to show that american iron what a real, live to ride, bike sounds and acts like. Ya a Triumph Scrambler for maybe monday through thursday.
