The RSVR is awesome, no other words describe, but with the K-Tec set up ohlins, the slipper clutch and radial brakes, its just begging to go faster and faster.

And i am getting older and older, and i just dont want to go that fast anymore.
(I know its me in control, but it think its just a matter of time..)

I have had a few Sm's in the past (KTM 625 and a Husky)

I loved the SM, but not the services and pain in the ass mileage.

So think either SM or SD is the way to go.

Also people are moaning about tank range? What sort of mileage are we looking at..(i have had an SP1, so am not fusses about massive range!!)

I want to tight twisties to be smiling, and not the flat out big curves.

All my mileage is fun miles, so thats what im hoping for.
