Mine with Leos:



I agree that they are very loud with and without dB-killers...

In matter of fact I've measured the dB values last year and the results were:

96,7 dB @ 4500 RPM with dB-killer
102 dB @ 4500 RPM without dB-killer

At same time we measured Duke with Akrapovic cans and the results were quite silmilar (Akras are bit louder without baffles):

95,2 dB @ 4500 RPM with dB-killer
103,4 dB @ 4500 RPM without dB-killer

If you compare the sound between Akras and Leos, I would say that Leos have much deeper sound....
