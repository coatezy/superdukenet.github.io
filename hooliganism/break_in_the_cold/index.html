We too are having a delightfully mild spell - in fact this has been a delightful winter so far - consequently, my bud and I took our bikes out yesterday for a nice afternoon ride -


I love my SD - of all the bikes I've owned this bike is the best - the sound, the mid range torque, the handling all is great -

At 70 I hope I can continue this for another 10 years. I'll take every day I can enjoy it to the fullest - today I/m going to ride the 690 enduro a bit but can't do much off road as the roads are pretty icy - don't need a crash to add to my otherwise achy body -

Enjoy the rest of winter for those in this hemisphere - spring is just around the corner and we've got to get things lined up for the riding season ahead.
