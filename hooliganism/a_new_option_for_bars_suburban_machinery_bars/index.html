A new option for bars- Suburban Machinery Bars

drswade

2010-07-25 05:20:00 UTC

I've been looking for some new bars. Getting down and pushing the bike away from me at the track was alittle too difficult for me because my hands were too up in the air. I like the reviews on this site about the Rizoma and the LSL flat bars. I decided to get the Suburban Machinery bars. They have more of a clipon feel to them without being completely hunched over. Almost like a clip on with a riser. I had to replace my throttle housing today (that was a b*tch) so I decided it was a good time to replace the bars to.
