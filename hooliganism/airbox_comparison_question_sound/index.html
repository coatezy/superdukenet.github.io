tripoddave

2012-06-10 00:46:00 UTC

I've run a modified stock airbox, no airbox at all and the DNA Stage III.
There's not much to choose between the DNA and no airbox: both sound awesome and massively loud but, weirdly, do not trip the noise meter as much on track days. If cost is an issue then the Rottweilers that Viking fitted will most probably be the best bet.

990 WFO

2012-06-10 01:14:00 UTC

I have the Motobox and cannot hear my FMF Apex cans (no baffles) at all under hard acceleration. I think all the boxes are going to sound roughly the same, the only variance is price and power. DNA is the most expensive and the most powerful box out there, but if you can't afford it the Motobox is a great kit and (with a set of $11 RC8 inlets) comes close to making the power a DNA box with stock inlets does.
