First Track Day

OldTLSDoug

2014-12-01 11:25:00 UTC

I Went to the track on Sunday and wow is all I can say. My SD really amazed me. Fast, turned well, brakes were awesome. Not bad. I was running Dunlop GPA's (33/23 psi hot) and they did the business. I could not believe how quickly and easily the thing changes direction. I also had installed the race rotors and EBC race pads. WOW! I had way more brake than stock. This thing is a wonderful track toy. I had no dragging issues (except my knees and that was ok).

The odd thing was on the track I would get an MTC failure, ABS failure and a check engine light. If I pulled off and cycled the ignition, it immediately cleared. I talked to a KTM mechanic at the track and he said it could be a map glitch. I will find out soon, I guess. The bike ran fine the TC and ABS continues to work fine. I did not have this issue on the street.

Anyway, the other thing was the speed and acceleration was awesome. I was getting places much faster. Going down the front straight there was a 30mph headwind and if you didn't stay tucked it would carry the front wheel all the way down the straight. Power is awesome to have on the racetrack. Woo Hoo!
