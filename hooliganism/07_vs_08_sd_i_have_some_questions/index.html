If you like Leo Vince's slip on, check out MotoMummy.com. The Ti or CF cans are $740. These cans drop about 12 lbs off the stock pipes.

Here is my bike with the CF Leos.

My 2008 really lugged in first with the stock 16/38 so I stepped to a 16/40 with the Akra's. It feels fantastic now. Here's a pic though the quality isn't as good as it could be. Kind of dirty. Too busy riding with all these 70 degree days here in Cali.

I did a lot of other settings to make it nicer for me. I am 6'3" and 210 so I set the suspension to the 34/37 psi on the tyres and the settings to the <90kg settings on the bottom of the seat. Made a huge difference for someone my size. It was a bit skittish before that.
