if you are on about the torque screws you have to cut a slice in to them to take off the ignition barrel and use a impact driver to unscrew,you may have to heat up the triple clamp where the threads are as they are thread locked in,i had to take my ignition barrel off a few weeks ago as the key would not turn in it propely and this is what i had to do...im going to the garage later and can take a pic of the slots i made in mine to get them out if it helps.you can buy new torque screws from ktm and when you tighten them back up the head of the bolt then snaps off as security to make it difficult to take ignition off so they cant remove steering lock if on,there's 2 screws under neath which you can unscrew and pull the lower part down and you can then start the bike like that...lol so not so secure after all...lol
