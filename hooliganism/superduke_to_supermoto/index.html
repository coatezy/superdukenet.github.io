matt_black

2011-06-16 19:54:00 UTC

I did it the other way round. I had a KTM 640 supermoto for hooning about London. In 4 years I achieved a constant total of 9 points on my license for the whole duration - I was always only one more penalty away from a driving ban. Each time one dropped off I got another. The Supermoto makes you ride like a total fool with no regard for the law, speeding or sense. It's fantastic! But it' s a terrible bike for open roads and any speeds over 80mph really. Vibrates so bad that your hands are always numb. And it runs out of puff very quickly. Superduke is a much, much better bike for nearly everything else. But I would have another like a shot if I could justify having another commuter bike to get to work on through the traffic and pot holes. Best London bike ever invented - easily.
