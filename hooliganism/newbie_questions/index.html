cjc

2008-01-20 20:28:00 UTC

The toolkit should be under the seat, it will fit in the compartment with the alarm, just. The KTM alarm is a META unit, which just plugs into the loom along with the seat switch and LED. This keeps the instalation and wiring neat. There is also a mounting bracket available for the KTM alarm, again keeping everything neat and tidy. The datatool is a good alarm, but your instalation won't be as neat and tidy as the KTM alarm.
