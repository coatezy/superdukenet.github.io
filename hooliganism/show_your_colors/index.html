Luxamor

2013-12-16 23:09:00 UTC

OK...I looked through these forums and did not see this topic discussed and I apologize if it has been.
In fact, I apologize if this seems like a stupid subject, but it is bothering me because my profession involves all things visual. And yeah, maybe I'm a bit OCD.
Here we go.
You have to admit that KTM orange is different.
It's not that I don't like it, but I haven't had to deal with it on my other bikes.
How the hell do you coordinate anything with this color?
I have always had success with black and red attire and helmets.
That's what I have now, but the more I consider getting serious about putting down money on the 1290 SDR, the more I think I'll look like a barfy color combo riding down the road.
I know...as long as I'm seen. Maybe I should go hi-viz, but OMG...hi-viz green and orange? whoa...
What, if anything, did the experienced KTM people do to resolve this strange predicament?
