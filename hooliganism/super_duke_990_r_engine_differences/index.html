The SDR from 2008-2011 have the uprated engine.
From 2012 the SDR was downgraded.
You can recognize a full power sdr from the Akrapovic headers, and the higher revlimit on the tacho (normal limit 9800, sdr limit 10.200)
The difference in headers (top one is SDR, bottom is normal sd)


And here is a test of the 08 SDR which sums up the engine mods:

From 2008 the SDR (single seater) and the uprated engine. The 2012 was a parts bin special (orange frame, dual seat), it has the SDR (single seat) geometery/suspension but has the standard engine and headers.

Read thread for an insight to some of the differences.
