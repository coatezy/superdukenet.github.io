I use the KTM factory tank bag and as pleased as I have ever been with the any tank bag arrangement. It fits great it sticks to the tank with little movemment. It is very easy to disconect from its base. I use a tank bag on every bike and every ride unless it is on the track. This may sound korny but on long ass freeway stomps the tank bag becomes a chest pillow and I go into the laying down forward mode of sleepy boring riding. The bag will come off and go back on in less than seven seconds, so I do not see how that could ruin the "Oh my bike is sooo pretty sitting next to that farmers pasture full of sheep", picture.
