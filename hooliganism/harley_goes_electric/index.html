I might have been in too much of a black and white/literal frame of mind when I made the comment about tuning.

I took tuning to mean the work that those with the smarts and experience to figure out how to modify the intake, electronics, exhaust, shock valving, etc. to improve the performance of a vehicle. I, and I'll bet most buyers, have no desire to do that.
