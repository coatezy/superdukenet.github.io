The best I can figure is that it is the sound of the air getting around the secondaries while the are closed. At low throttle primary butterfly openings try to pull air around the secondaries and it produces the chirps and tweets that you here when you are trying to be a good citizen. They were there with the air box but muffled.
Someone please correct me if I'm wrong.

My bet is that the new 'flies that Aaron has made should be free flowing enough to put an end to the sounds of "Disney birds" flying around our heads while still keeping the worst of the volume down and keeping the beneficial airflow affects.

I love the overall differences the box has made. The chirps and tweets are a small price to pay for the gains but if the new 'flies can help well....
