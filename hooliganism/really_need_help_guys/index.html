990 WFO

2011-08-24 15:26:00 UTC

If the lock pin engages a pocket, you cant slip the top tripple clamp up. The pin will be engaged.
If it is, then slide both forks and the lower tripple down, freeing up the top clamp from the steering stem.
You will need to support the bike, till the top clamp is reinstalled.
