Kenan was a true pleasure to watch when he came up to supersport he was fast and clean and won with a riding style that favored high speed through the turns. I had no doubt that he was destined for MotoGP greatness. He went up to superbikes and he seemed to get way too aggresive and lost much of what made him fast to begin with. He went back down to sport and was a pleasure to watch again. When entering the Moto2 series he went back to the backing it in and forcing his way through to the point that I thought he may be hangin with Semincelli. I do not appreciate that "racecraft" and feel that it belongs at the club level at best. The race of the weekend for me was the second race of the Daytona Sportbike in Wisconsin on Sunday. Seven guys bar to bar, thirteen laps of pure racing pleasure. I could not help but to root for Alex Espargaro in Moto 2 him and his bro make racing all the better to watch. I for the life of me do not understand why Kenan chose to go the Moto 2 route instead of staying in World Supersport. Mika Kalio and Alex DiAngelis need to step it up a bit.
