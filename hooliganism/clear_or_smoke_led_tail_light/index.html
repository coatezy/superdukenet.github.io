Yep, go for the smoked version!

I got mine from http://2a4.fr
Getting the taillight off is annoying (to say the least). After trying al kinds of tools I've eventually sawn a 10mm wrench in half.
The tailligt I got came with the right connector for the light and bullet connectors for the blinkers. The blinkers don't come with a ground wire, the ground wire from the taillight is used for that. The blinker wires did not come labled so I had to try which color was which side.
The flasher relay is behind the headlight mask and is pretty small. The new was almost twice the size. It came with the right connector so it was a straight swap (and a bit of a fiddle to get is back in the place where the original relay was located).
