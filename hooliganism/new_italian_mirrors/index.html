New Italian Mirrors

speedmotorrad

2011-10-15 02:58:00 UTC

Just got a set of mirrors from these guys... http://www.moto-science.com/

Tons of mirrors on that site, got these... http://www.moto-science.com/product/duc ... lloy-black

Similar shape to a Rizoma Radial Naked. Thicker body than the Rizomas, and not quite the quality, but half the price. Came in quickly, arrived today, 4 days after I placed my order.

Installed them tonight, or at least the left one. The mounting bolt is a couple of millimeters too long on the right, the mirror mount on that side also is the clamp for the master cylinder and not as deep as the left side, will have to pick up a shorter bolt tomorrow.

Here's a pic in the garage, they don't stick out as much as the stock ones, but adjust well, the sloping underside lines up nicely with my arm and gives a good field of view. They have a matte black finish that matches the tank perfectly, think they'll work well and definitely look a lot better. The only thing I don't like is that they have black print embossed on the bottom edge of the mirror glass, but it's pretty much the area where you look at your jacket sleeve so not too big a deal.
