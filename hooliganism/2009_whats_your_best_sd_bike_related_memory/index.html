2009 Whats your best SD/bike related memory?

Superdan

2009-12-25 13:44:00 UTC

So what your best memory of the biking in 2009 as this year draws to a close?

Did you get your knee down? first wheelie? just had a fun day out with the SD guys??

For me it was Cadwell when a few of from here turned up, after a good year or so of chasing Sabre and watching him whoop me on track, I overtook him when he was not paying attention and had the best lap ever playing catch me if you can, you did catch me Ginge but it was bloody fun and going round lap after lap with me on your back tyre was definately the best day of the year by far.

My riding has come along this year, and I got that elusive right knee down.

No stopping me in 2010 trackday central.
