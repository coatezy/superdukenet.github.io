yeah sorry about the GPS, but half of northern fooking france was shut!!
great trip..good to follow stumpy around most of the time , great track, letter on the way to KTM about shit bikes, 4 out of 4 had probs.
but contrarary to Weeksys Nurburgring hell, i loved it...me and pablo were supposed to wait, but the red mist came down and we went for it , and it was as sweet a lap as i have done there previously.
Spa was good apart from Pablos off and me having to dress a raw piece of meat that once was his right hand, and put up with him snoring like a harpooned whale. Next time get out of france as quickly as poss and head for the belgium and germany borders for some twisty fun...and to stop at less fooking cake shops to satisfy weeksy's cream craving...but a good trip highly recommended...great bunch of tossers..
