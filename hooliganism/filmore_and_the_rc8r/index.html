2011-07-12 00:27:00 UTC

I think you'll find that it was not a showroom stock RC8R but in fact a track preped factory track bike. KTM will be selling them to the general public for $19,999.

Motorcyclist mag just did a write up on it. Straight out of the box compared to the standard 2011 RC8R at Chuckwalla, the track bike was 2 seconds a lap faster. Granted they come from KTM with Dunlop race slicks as standard.

The major differences are upgraded suspension, wheels, bodywork (without headlight opening), levers, rearsets, thinner head gaskets, cams (I believe) and exhaust. All engine mods equal and additional 10hp at the wheel over the stock 2011 RC8R. Oh, it's 47lbs lighter than the stock bike as well (mostly due to the pipe and lack of street equipment...presumably).
