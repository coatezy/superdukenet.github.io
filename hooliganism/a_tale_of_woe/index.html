I had the same problem but found out that I was being an idiot and caused it myself.

Check this to be sure if you're not like me (hopefully you're not).

On the negative side of the battery there SHOULD BE 2 cables attached... one thick and one very very small. That small cable can easily get pushed into the batter case when re-installing the battery and you would never know its there. If its not hooked up the bike will start up fine, fun off of the power of the battery while it lasts but wont recharge.

I know its a long shot and it sounds like you would have seen that by now but just make sure TWO wires are hooked up to the negative side.
