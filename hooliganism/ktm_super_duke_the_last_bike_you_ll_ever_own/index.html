Hell yeah!! I LOVE my SD, I mean i fooking LOVE IT! I've had it for nearly 2 years, I'm still becoming a better rider just so I can do this bike justice. I commute on it whenever i can and so far I've done 29000 miles on it in all that time.

rackdays planned, trips booked, mods made and more to come. I cant get enough of this amazing machine...why the hell wold I trade it in.

For the first time ever, I dont have envy of anyone else on the road. Sure there are other amazing bikes out there and if I had the chance to own more than one I"d got for the SDR/a sensible commuter/a ZX Fireblade and a fun single...oh and maybe a nice BMW HP2 but anyway....for now the SD is all these in ONE!!

The love affair continues and show no sign of ending....I just hope it doesnt dump me!!
