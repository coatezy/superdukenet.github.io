He guys it has been a while.I just had to share this with you guys.As a lot of you know I have my RC8R all tricked out.
But I got a deal you won't believe .I met a couple that were in there 70's.They one a 08 GSXR600 in 08 It had a football graphics Trust me it was ugly.
I peeled off the wrap which had protected the paint.So under the wrap was a beautful stock paint job .The bike had not been started in several years.

I knew I had to do some things to get it back but I payed 3k for a brand new 08 GSXR 600 with 1 mile and never had been ridden. I will be the first to ride it. The dealer said it was worth 7-8 k It looks like it did in 08 like it came out of a time warp. I am a ktm guy and love the V twins .I can't wait to track it just for fun after i put about 700 miles I will most likey sell it or try to get another duke. I really miss mine I had to sell it and now with this bike I can sell it to get another one. I had to buy this bike 3k for a brand new bike never ridden i would be nuts not to buy it. And I will be able to get another duke for the price I will sell my GSXR 600. pretty cool I think
