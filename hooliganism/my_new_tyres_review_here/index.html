hi fellas , just fitted a pair of maxxis supermax supersport tyres to my superduke after running off a pair of pirelli diablos which lasted a very short expensive life. i am happy to say these maxxis jobs are brilliant , did the sensible scrubbing off warmed them up and to be honest ive nailed the bike through the yorkshire dales twisties and reallly hammered it like ive never before and it has gripped gripped gripped , £160 fitted ! i had these on my previous cb 1000r naked bike and they were faultless then so i thought why not on me sduke. you have got to try these at the price seen em on ebay for £150 delivered. always had this comfort thing of having to have pirellis on but i have finally let go and have full confidence it these .

I'm also using these and i agree they are very good tyres, i've had them on previous bikes too. i got mine off ebay £150 delivered, and paid Mark at m&m in stamford £6 per wheel fitted balanced and old tyres disposed of
one thing i found is that they're very slippy before scrubbing in, i was spinning the wheel up my road when i set off on my scrub in run, i took it easy for the first ride but unfortunatley i slipped of on a roundabout, thank fook for R&G's! no damage to the bike. they're well scrubbed in now and have only little chicken rings on the back (like 5mm ish).
