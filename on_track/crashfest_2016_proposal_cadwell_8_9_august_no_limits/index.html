Crashfest 2016 proposal - Cadwell, 8/9 August, No Limits

Scotty

2016-03-31 07:49:00 UTC

A trifle late for an announcement I know, but I booked onto these dates yesterday, a double-header on Monday & Tuesday, nice easy day to get up there on the Sunday.
I've just read through all the 2015 Crashfest thread and find it hard to believe that nothing similar has been proposed for this year. I guess people move on, Klinck was the main mover and shaker for last year but he's left the fold, in bike if not in spirit.
I'm already doing the event with a bunch of mates from Wiltshire and South West Bikers so I won't be alone if no fellow SDers come along but it would be good to rally the troops for a bit of a get-together. Besides, I now have Shadowman's former bike and I'm itching to take it round my favourite circuit.
So, is anyone interested?
