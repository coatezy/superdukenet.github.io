Well Dave, Mike, Marc, Ravi (my brother) and myself all had an excellent day at Donington yesterday. Weather was dry and warm all day, the sun even came out in the afternoon. Had a chat to one the marshall's who advised me not to use my quickshifter on the start/finsih straight as it was causing a backfire, and also to drop the rev's as i went pass the sound meter, I did and no further problems. A very well organised day as well, and not too busy either, plenty of space out on track. If only they all cost £25
