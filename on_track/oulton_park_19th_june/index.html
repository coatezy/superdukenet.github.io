Viking

2012-06-19 23:45:00 UTC

Good day.

Things i learned..........
CompK's are actually pretty good, wear beautifully, but move about quite a bit. Had a few proper slides on the throttle round cascades and another round island. Normal little slides over hilltop and deep leap. Feels nice though, really gentle and controlable with no snapping, just breaks traction gradually and predictably. Dunno what i'm going to do tyre wise for the races though.

Adding 10mm the the wheelbase doesnt do quite as much as you'd think.

Racechrono works better than Trackmaster

Having the van is loads better than the car and trailer




Set a goal of a 1.53 lap and to tidy up my lines. Usualy moan from me about loads of traffic, but got a few clear laps in the odd session. Still finding my feet with these tyres so not fully got trust on the front through druids and wasnt running it in fast enough. Being to feely and gentle.

Got a 1min 54.004. Dammit. All best sectors on that session add up to 1min 53.70 so the time is there. Losing out big time on the sportsbikes though, they can drag so much time on the straights.

Just usual speel from me then. Took another step forward, learned more stuff about the bike etc, knocked 0.5 sec off my pb lap time, and didnt crash............but still annoyed at myself coz i didnt crack into the 53's (bloody .004!!!) and/or make a big enough step forward.

Good seeing the boys again and meetin Stalker too.
