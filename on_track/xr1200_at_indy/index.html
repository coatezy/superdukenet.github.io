XR1200 at Indy

drswade

2011-08-29 18:58:00 UTC

Big congrats to my buddy Tyler O'Hara for his first place and dual podiums at Indy on those miserably heavy HDs!
Watching those guys throw them around reminds me of racing a SD. I know the SD is an easier ride but both bikes really stand out on the track as being peculiar to road racing.

Funny how the commentators kept talking about everyone but Tyler even though he led most of the races. That's what happens when you don't have a PR team, I guess?

Also my friend Shelina Moreda got a chance to race those beasts too and was acknowledged as being the first female to ride a moto at Indianapolis!!!
