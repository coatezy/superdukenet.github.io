Thanks for your kind comments.

A great track for sure, my favourite. Harry went on to Parc Algar and is there for 3 days. I was there last month. He should come back with some more awesome pictures as the place lends it's self to dramatic shots.

The photographer said he enjoyed shooting the SD as it was different and he thought the orange(s) stood out well.

The track was resurfaced last Sept. When I was there in Oct. it was still very "green". Now it's perfect, with the new surface they have removed just about all the bumps, not that it was bad before.

Over time they will return, it's a F1 test track and they race other cars there a fair bit.
