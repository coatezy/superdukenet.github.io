Viking

2012-06-01 19:36:00 UTC

Few points on braking and entry.

Been having problems with it lifting the rear off the deck if i brake as late and hard as i want to. I'm editing a Donny vid and that will show it.
He just slowed down the speed i apply them and how much force i use through the braking zone. So its more a squeeze of the lever that gets harder then tapers off transitioning to turn in....where as before it was a grab, lift rear, release lever a bit to recover it, then back on hard at end of braking zone making entry very busy. I could get away with that in the cars but the bike didnt like it. In the cars you'd brake harder at the start of the braking coz thats when you had more downforce so it braked better then would snatch a wheel once the speed came down. I've to clamp the tank a lil bitty more so when it does lift it isn't instantly trying to rotate round the headstock and i can hold it straight.

On entry i though i was doing it right. Full gas, chin on tank, arse cheek off, clamp tank, brake and pop up, head and shoulder in on tip in and we're away. Problem was at tip in the thing started moving so i had to pause to let it settle before getting to apex where as i should be on the gas as soon as i know the bike is making the apex. I was doing as all the above, then just at tip in i was moving my ass even further......like taking a second bite at it. No idea i was doing that.

I'll get the donny vid up in a day or so. Just working on whoring the absolute living shit out it.
