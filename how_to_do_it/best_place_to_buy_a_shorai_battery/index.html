Yep, I think it is. The product code was LFX18A1-BS12

I did ring the dealer and had a long chat with him and also to check whether this was the right battery model as I don't trust website guides that much and didn't want to order and get the wrong one delivered.

What I was told was this amongst other things. The replacement battery terminals are very near the same place as the standard battery if you use the foam packing correctly. I was worried that if the battery was smaller I would have trouble connecting all the leads to the terminals - there are a lot of them and with not much additional length to play with.

You cannot use a conventional trickle charger, (like and Optimate), with a Shorai battery because many of them have an electronic programme that reverses the polarity for a time to help revive conventional batteries - this would effectively destroy a Shorai. That is why it is also OK to charge a Shorai with a conventional charger - as long as it is only a simple one and not electronic with diagnostic ability.

He advised that if there is a constant drain on the battery in storage - which there is on my SD because of the alarm - then I would need to get the Shorai trickle charger because if you every run a Shorai flat is is ruined, you cannot revive them. Not sure if this is just a good sales message to sell chargers but I din't want to take the risk and it makes sense to have the ability to trickle charge as I cannot get out and ride as much and often as I used to.
