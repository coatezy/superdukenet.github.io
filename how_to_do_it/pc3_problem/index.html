Hi, I have an 08 model also with a PCIII. With the original exhaust and the o2 sensor plugs in they run overly rich on the 501 and the 0 map (all the settings in pciii on 0).
I say your cat is already clogged up. Ditch the exhaust or take to a dyno to get the map sorted.
To give you an example at 3000 rpm 2% trottle my pciii map is now -10 this just to tell you how rich it was. The o2 sensor plugs set the fuel map fixed in low rpm low trottle depending on the resistor in them. Somehow on the 08 model this is very rich.
Hope this helps.
