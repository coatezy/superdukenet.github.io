Scotts steering damper on '08 SD

wintermute

2009-10-22 22:25:00 UTC

I installed a Scotts steering damper on my '08 SD today. Because I have the KTM bar risers installed, I had to ask Scotts to send my a longer tower pin. They were awesome, getting it to me for no additional charge in 3 days. The installation is straightforward, with the only tricky part being the need to use a dab of grease to hold the support bushing to the frame while installing a Nylok nut below the frame tubes. I lost one Nylok nut along the way.

I set the low speed damping to 8 clicks back from full hard, as recommended as a first setting, and left the high speed damping and damping sweep unchanged for now. The effect of the damper is profound. It dramatically improves the sense of stability and confidence on the SD, especially on a bumpy road at speeds over 80 MPH. I was able to blast down some of my usual roads much faster with greater confidence because of the quiet steering produced by the damper. I am very impressed with the quality of the Scotts hardware, and with the effectiveness of the engineering. Highly recommended!
