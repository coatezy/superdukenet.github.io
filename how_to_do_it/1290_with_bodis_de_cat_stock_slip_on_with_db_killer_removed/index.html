1290 with bodis de-cat, stock slip on with db-killer removed

driftkr6l

2015-05-21 18:52:00 UTC

Hey guys, after installing the bodis de-cat the bike really started to sound like it should, but it wasn't enough for me, so I read on here that you can remove the db-killer pipe from the stock muffler so I did.

Here are a couple of action pics as well as a video sound clip.

I tried to cut it out without disassembling the muffler, but it was a PITA, you cant really see what you are doing, how deep you are cutting or where, and once you stop, its hard to re start in the right place as you can not see what you are doing. So I just disassembled the end cap, to me this was the easier approach.



Once thats done, cut to the left of the weld as in the pic below


Once you cut through, the pipe will slip out like this




After that clean up the cut with a file, pound the cap back on, put new rivets in and enjoy the new look and sound.
