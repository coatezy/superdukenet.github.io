2011-12-03 17:54:00 UTC

Replaced a bent frame on my 08SD last year. Took some pointers from Paul with his build. Bike bench which raises the bike from the floor for one. Other major ,would do it again item, is to use a motor stand to support the motor at trear down. This "A" frame stand supports the motor that stays put on the bike bench. Every thing can be removed / replace with the stand still connected to the motor. The frame can even be pivioted into place by using one rear frame bolt. The sub frame can also be piovoted into place. I even left the air box and TB inplace with wire harness and TB cabiling. I did all of this by my self. I will have to post some pictures of what I did to give back to this very helpfull site. Some times I might come across as a know it all...not the intent...I just want to share my knowlege or lack of it ..Got more than 300K miles on sport bikes and do most of my own wrenching. Will be 60 in May and started the bike bug before teen age. Will post later,,,going out now on my 300KTM 2 smoker for a 40 mile loop.
