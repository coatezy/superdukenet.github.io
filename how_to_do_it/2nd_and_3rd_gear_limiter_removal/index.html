2nd and 3rd gear limiter removal

9nine0

2008-05-19 23:26:00 UTC

Step one, remove lower chin spoiler to expose connector. The nuetral light, 2nd and 3rd gear limiter connector is the one located to the far left with the black female mated to the white male end. The wire colors from the sensor (motorcycle side) are yellow with green tracer, green, and blue with a red tracer.



Unplug this connector and cut/unplug the yellow with green and blue with red. DO NOT remove / cut the green wire. This is your "N" light.



Tape off or isolate the two cut or unplugged wires on the motorcycle side. Here I just taped them off seperate from the other side of the wires I cut.



Finally tape up the remaining two cut wires and rest of the wire loom.



Reconnect the connector and install the lower chin spoiler. Enjoy full power in 2nd and 3rd gears
